_______________________________________________________________________________________<br />
Tony Lit, MD of Sunrise Radio is delighted to announce that Sunrise Radio 963/972 will be the official radio sponsors for an intimate musical evening with Ustad Rahat Fateh Ali Khan live at the Grosvenor House Hotel, London on the 12th October.</p>
<p>Lit said &#8220;We are delighted to be working with Sonas Haute Couture promoters of this amazing audience with Rahat, he is without a doubt one of the outstanding singers of his generation.  This will be a magical evening where those who are lucky enough to get a ticket will be treated to Rahat&#8217;s music up-close and personal while enjoying Madhus finest Indian cuisine.  At Sunrise we&#8217;re in the music and entertainment business and it doesn&#8217;t get much better than this.&#8221;</p>
<p>Kej Jivraj of Sonas Haute Couture also commented saying &#8220;After Ustad Rahat Fateh Ali Khan&#8217;s sell-out UK Concerts over the past few years, we now bring you a very special one off VIP Event at an exclusive venue with a superb four course dining experience.  The finest Indian cuisine by Madhus and music by the indisputable musical genius Ustad Rahat Fateh Ali Khan, the perfect  combination for an unforgettable evening.  We&#8217;re also pleased to have Sunrise Radio 963/972 as our radio sponsors.  Those attending will be in for an amazing treat.</p>
<p>Ustad Rahat Fateh Ali Khan live at the Grosvenor on Sunday 12th October 2014 at 18:00. For tickets and information call  07956 900 373 or  07958 339 997, or contact: <a href="mailto:info@voodooentertainment.co.uk">info@voodooentertainment.co.uk</a></p>

<p>PRESS RELEASE</p>
<p>Sunrise Radio 963/972 is delighted to announce its sponsorship of the 2014 Pakistan Achievement Awards UK &amp; Europe taking place on Friday 10<sup>th</sup> October at the prestigious Marriot, Grosvenor Square, London.</p>
<p>Sunrise Radio MD Tony Lit says “Sunrise Radio is at the hub of the Asian community and it gives one great pleasure to see individuals from these communities  grow and achieve excellence.  On the 10<sup>th</sup> of October we will be honouring the achievements of the Pakistani community and sharing these with our audience.  I feel it’s important to relay these success stories to our young listeners, giving them motivation and showing that anything is possible with a can do attitude.”</p>
<p>Mr Haq Chairman of the Pakistan Achievement Awards UK &amp; Europe commented “We are very pleased to have Sunrise Radio 963/972 as the sponsor for our 2014 Awards.  Sunrise Radio through its music unites the Asian community which is diverse in language and culture.  We’re looking forward to a great evening and I’m glad that the achievements of the Pakistani community will be shared with Sunrise Radio’s listeners.”</p>
<p>The Pakistan Achievement Awards UK &amp; Europe take place on Friday 10<sup>th</sup> October at the Marriot, Grosvenor Square, London. For information visit <a href="http://pakawards.org.uk" target="_blank">pakawards.org.uk</a></p>
