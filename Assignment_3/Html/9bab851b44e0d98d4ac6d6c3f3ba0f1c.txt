<h3>
Fashion, Poetry and Music.&nbsp;</h3>
<br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<b>These past couple of weeks, we've been following some incredible visionaries in fashion, music and poetry.</b></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<b>We caught up with young pioneers chasing dreams and making waves. Each of them, whether its in the fashion retail industry, poetry scene and music landscape, have made a solid name for themselves despite being young.</b></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<b><br /></b></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<b>We took to the East Coast, eThekwini, eGagasini to hang out with Kundai Vera, known on these streets as Project Kay as he filmed his new music video. As music producer, visual effects artist and of course music artist, Project Kay exhibits raw talent and skill for enticing the audience. What a pleasure to watch him in action.</b></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<b><br /></b></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<b>The Amazing Slum Intellect, an Eastern Cape born, but Durban-bred poet took a few minutes out of is time to share with us his rise in the fast growing industry of poetry. We were awed at his humility and even more impressed by the rhymes he spit for us.</b></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<b><br /></b></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<b>Last, but definitely not least, trendy guys from fashion capitals Joburg and Cape Town, who you'll know as the owners and founders of Cove Fashion chat to us about their online retail store. Access to fashion should have no limits and these guys provide just that.</b></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<b><br /></b></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<b>Enjoy!</b></div>

<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 150%;">Tell us a little about yourself.</span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 150%;">Its nothing interesting , I&#8217;m from
Lusikisiki but grew up in Durban started writing when I was in grade six , it was
some pretty weak stuff though. Until I met this guy called Mystic who I becam close friends with that&#8217;s when I grew a lot as a writer&nbsp;</span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">
<br />
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 150%;">How did the name Amazing The Slum Intellect
come about?</span></b><br />
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 150%;">&nbsp;My rap name before was Mini Virus, when I met
mystic in boarding school I rapped for him and he thought it was amazing and so
he named me Amazing. The Slum Intellect I named myself,
so it was Amazing The Slum Intellect a mixture of what of what Mystic thought
and what I think of myself.</span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 150%;">&nbsp; </span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 150%;">Where or who do you draw your
inspiration from? </span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 150%;">From everything, stuff that<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>I have gone through, things that I see and
generally from other Artist. If your a poet and I enjoy your poetry, then I am
going to draw inspiration from your technique as a writer and try to improve
myself.</span></b><br />
</div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 150%;">Do you have any great writers that
you admire?</span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 150%;">Frantz Fanon I enjoy his work,
George Orwell and Mongane Serote. Myself also, I would like&nbsp; to believe I&#8217;m great writer.</span></b><br />
</div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 150%;">What made you enter the Human
Rights Day event that you actually won?</span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 150%;">I got an invite on Facebook <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp;</span>to go check it out. I entered I was not even
aware that it was a competition, all I&nbsp; wanted to do was perform.</span></b><br />
</div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 150%;">Do you let other people read your
poetry? </span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 150%;">I do not let them read, they can
hear it I can recite anything for anyone. If I give them my book there is a
whole lot of scratched out lines , I don&#8217;t want people seeing stuff that I wrote
and did not think it was good enough.</span></b><br />
</div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 150%;">Where would you like to go with
your poetry?</span></b><br />
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 150%;">&nbsp;I&#8217;d like to believe that I will be
a established poet one day. In the sense that I can say stuff and influence
people on how they think and how they view the world, I hope it is not just for
entertainment.</span></b><br />
</div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 150%;">Advice on how people can embrace
poetry?</span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 150%;">I think poetry starts with music;
most people say it&#8217;s the other way round. In this day of age not a lot of people
listen to poetry. You really have to start from the music and then introduce
poetry through music. Which is why I rap and I&#8217;m with a production called No way
Out Productions because we are trying to push the message through the music. Once
someone has gotten into the music, then they can phase the beats out, phase the
instruments out and listen to the words.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span></b><br />
<br />
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 150%;"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">Link to The Amazing Slum Intellect Reciting one of his poems. </span></span></b><br />
