Nick Castillo

   Nick Castillo is a sports journalist who is currently studying
   journalism at the University of Texas at Austin. He is currently
   working as a Senior Sports Writer, covering Texas volleyball, at The
   Daily Texan. He is also an intern for HornsDigest.com. He is skilled in
   reporting, interviewing, blogging, editing and in social media.
