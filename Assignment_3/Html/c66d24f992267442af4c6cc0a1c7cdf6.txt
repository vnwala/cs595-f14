<h1>Stuff you don't need? Freegle it!</h1>

	<p>Don’t throw it away – give it away on Freegle! You might not need that old sofa or wheelbarrow any more – but there might be someone just round the corner who does. Or if there’s something you’d like, someone nearby might have one.</p>

	<p>Lurking in sheds, attics and cupboards all over the UK are items that are too good to throw away, but are not loved or needed anymore.  Freegle online groups can help!</p>

<p>
	<i>
		Remember, if you can't find what you need through Freegle, then please shop online through
		<a href="/give-as-you-live/">Give as you Live</a> and raise funds for Freegle, without costing you a penny extra.
	</i>
</p>

<p>
	There are 1,858,541 Freegle members in
	399 reuse groups all over the UK<span id="showRecentMessagesLine"> 
		- <a href="/realtimemap.php">see photos of recently Freegled items and a live map of new messages</a></span>.
</p>
