<div style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: #ff0000; font-size: 24px;"><strong>City changes water disinfectant process for one month</strong></span></div>
<p>The City of Tyler will begin converting its disinfection process to free chlorine for a period of approximately one month beginning Oct. 7 and ending on Nov. 6. Generally, there are no noticeable changes in water quality as a result of this temporary conversion.&nbsp; However, some individuals may notice taste and odor changes and a slight discoloration to the water.&nbsp;</p>
