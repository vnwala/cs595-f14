Entertainment Law, Talent and Literary Representation

   Matthew Valentinas is a licensed Massachusetts attorney who represents
   screenwriters, producers, directors, actors, and production companies
   in all forms of entertainment matters.

   Prior to returning to his native Boston, Matthew worked in
   entertainment business affairs in New York and Los Angeles. Matthew
   holds a BFA in film and television production from New York
   University’s Tisch School of the Arts and a Juris Doctorate from
   Suffolk University Law School in Boston with a focus on intellectual
   property and corporate finance.

   The Law Office of Matthew Valentinas offers a free and confidential
   consultation with competitively priced fee and percentage agreements.
   Inquiries from emerging talent and recent graduates are welcome.
