[6]Tag - Facts About My Books

   So, because I don't write that many reviews(I need to make a schedule),
   I figured it would be fun to throw some tags in the mix as well. This
   is the first one. I saw this floating around on some of the blogs I
   follow and I thought it would be fun to answer this questions.
   The original tag is by [7]Book11Lover on Youtube
   1. How many books are in your collection?
   I do not have a serious count for this. I have an app on my phone which
   I used back in May to scan every single book I own. Or at least the
   ones I had in my house. There are also a bunch of them at my sisters
   and a few I left at my parents' house when I moved out. And I figured
   that since I scanned them I bought about 20 books, which brings the
   total of books I own around 300 right now. I have actually banned
   myself from buying more at the moment, I need to actually read a bunch
   of the books I own.

[25]Review - Artemis Fowl

   “Confidence is ignorance. If you're feeling cocky, it's because there's
   something you don't know.”
   [26][Colfer%252C-Eoin---Artemis-Fowl.jpg]
   ★ ★ ★ ★
   [27]The Book Depository | [28]GoodReads
   I have waited writing this review until I finished the series. I did a
   few weeks ago, so now you will get a review.  Friends of mine have told
   me to read it before and I figured why not. I devoured this first
   book(that is all broker and in pieces, but it looks amazing!) in a day.
   I loved it. I wanted more, but I was getting these books from my
   library and it took some time for all of them to arrive. The last one
   of the series I was able to borrow from a friend to read while on
   holiday.
   I think this is a series that every child should read. It is filled
   with action and humour. Give it to your kids, your nieces and your
   nephews. I am positive they will love the series.
   There are 4 stars for this first book, but I rated these books from 2-4
   stars. I would give the overal series somewhere between 3 and 4 stars.

[65]Hello

   My name is Rachel and I am currently 25 years old. English may not be
   my first language, but it is probably my favourite. I was born and
   raised in the Netherlands and started reading at a very young age. I
   love escaping in a world that a writer creates and just getting
   overwhelmed with feelings.
   I started reading in English when I was 11 years old and right now it
   is my favourite to read in. I cannot explain why, but somehow (for a
   non native speaker) things sound better in English. Things get lost in
   translation. I do enjoy reading books from Dutch writers in Dutch
   though. Otherwise I just prefer to read a book in English.
   I can't tell you what the future of this blog will be like, but I will
   just post my reviews here. You may or may not agree with me. That is
   okay. Please do leave a message in the comments for others to read,
   maybe you will inspire people to read. Brilliant.
   It is also possible that there will be some other posts on this blog. I
   also really enjoy watching movies and tv shows. I enjoy cooking, but I
   am terrible at that. And since a month or so, I also really enjoy
   knitting. And finally, I love photography and DIY. So who knows, maybe
   you will also see some updates about the projects I have going on.
   I hope you will stop by my blog every now and then and give me some
   feedback about how I'm doing.
