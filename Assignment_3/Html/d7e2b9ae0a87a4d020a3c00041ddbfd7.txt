<h2>Welcome</h2>

<p><img style="margin-left: 15px; float: right;" src="images/jules_may2013.jpg" alt="Jules Bennett" title="Jules Bennett" border="0" width="250" height="375">We all have passions, right? I'm one of the lucky ones who turned my passion into a career. Writing has always been a nice escape from reality and, let's face it, sometimes we all need to get away... even if the trip is in our own head.</p>

<p>My other passions? So glad you asked. I love my husband and girls more than anything, they're the only things in my life I won't sell for a good pair of shoes or a nice handbag.</p>

<p>Speaking of shoes and purses, they are two more passions of mine. I feel I can wear anything if I have the right accessories. And, yes, books are most definitely an accessory.</p>

<p>I love to hear from readers! Snail mail me at PO Box 396 Minford, Oh 45653 or e-mail me at <a href="mailto:authorjules@gmail.com">authorjules@gmail.com</a> and we'll chat!</p>

<p>Happy Reading!</p>
<p>Jules</p>
