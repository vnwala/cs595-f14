				<div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
			<p>Boy oh boy, where do I start.</p>
<ul>
<li>I was born on September 2nd 1985, I weighed 7.5 pounds. My parents named my Robert. I went to school and did alright, I got mostly B&#8217;s and C&#8217;s. Graduated high school in the 50th percentile. Went to college for 4 years, took out student loans and graduated with $29,400 of debt. I was very nervous about my future but thankfully my job hunt lasted only 4 months. I was fortunate enough to get a desk job where I basically look at numbers on a computer screen all day. (I love it) I work about 40 hours a week and occasionally work overtime on the weekends. I make about $45,000 a year right now and I&#8217;m very grateful.</li>
</ul>
<div id="attachment_48" style="width: 310px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://averagelifegoals.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/ID-100266022.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-48" src="http://averagelifegoals.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/ID-100266022-300x300.jpg" alt="Image courtesy of http://www.freedigitalphotos.net/images/view_photog.php?photogid=6851 at FreeDigitalPhotos.net" width="300" height="300" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Image courtesy of jesadaphorn at FreeDigitalPhotos.net</p></div>
<ul>
<li>I live in a decent one story house in a suburban neighborhood, there&#8217;s a couple oil stains in the driveway from the leaking &#8217;05 Corolla. I have a wife, and two kids. One boy and one girl, and we&#8217;re considering having another one. My wife stays home and takes care of the kids who aren&#8217;t yet in school. We enjoy sitting around the house and watching television.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>My hobbies consist of watching NFL games on sunday afternoons, watching television game shows and watching Brad Pitt movies. I have a gym membership that I&#8217;ve used maybe 4 times in the last year. Sometimes my friends will come over and watch TV with me, we&#8217;ll usually order a pizza from little caesars and drink some soda&#8217;s. My life is pretty much awesome.</li>
</ul>
<p>If there is anything else you&#8217;d like to know, please let me know by leaving a comment.</p>
