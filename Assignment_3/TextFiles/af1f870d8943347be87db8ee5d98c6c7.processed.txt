   Tony Lit, MD of Sunrise Radio is delighted to announce that Sunrise
   Radio 963/972 will be the official radio sponsors for an intimate
   musical evening with Ustad Rahat Fateh Ali Khan live at the Grosvenor
   House Hotel, London on the 12th October.

   Lit said “We are delighted to be working with Sonas Haute Couture
   promoters of this amazing audience with Rahat, he is without a doubt
   one of the outstanding singers of his generation.  This will be a
   magical evening where those who are lucky enough to get a ticket will
   be treated to Rahat’s music up-close and personal while enjoying Madhus
   finest Indian cuisine.  At Sunrise we’re in the music and entertainment
   business and it doesn’t get much better than this.”

   Kej Jivraj of Sonas Haute Couture also commented saying “After Ustad
   Rahat Fateh Ali Khan’s sell-out UK Concerts over the past few years, we
   now bring you a very special one off VIP Event at an exclusive venue
   with a superb four course dining experience.  The finest Indian cuisine
   by Madhus and music by the indisputable musical genius Ustad Rahat
   Fateh Ali Khan, the perfect  combination for an unforgettable evening.
   We’re also pleased to have Sunrise Radio 963/972 as our radio
   sponsors.  Those attending will be in for an amazing treat.

   Ustad Rahat Fateh Ali Khan live at the Grosvenor on Sunday 12th October
   2014 at 18:00. For tickets and information call  07956 900 373 or
   07958 339 997, or contact: [16]info@voodooentertainment.co.uk

   PRESS RELEASE

   Sunrise Radio 963/972 is delighted to announce its sponsorship of the
   2014 Pakistan Achievement Awards UK & Europe taking place on Friday
   10^th October at the prestigious Marriot, Grosvenor Square, London.

   Sunrise Radio MD Tony Lit says “Sunrise Radio is at the hub of the
   Asian community and it gives one great pleasure to see individuals from
   these communities  grow and achieve excellence.  On the 10^th of
   October we will be honouring the achievements of the Pakistani
   community and sharing these with our audience.  I feel it’s important
   to relay these success stories to our young listeners, giving them
   motivation and showing that anything is possible with a can do
   attitude.”

   Mr Haq Chairman of the Pakistan Achievement Awards UK & Europe
   commented “We are very pleased to have Sunrise Radio 963/972 as the
   sponsor for our 2014 Awards.  Sunrise Radio through its music unites
   the Asian community which is diverse in language and culture.  We’re
   looking forward to a great evening and I’m glad that the achievements
   of the Pakistani community will be shared with Sunrise Radio’s
   listeners.”

   The Pakistan Achievement Awards UK & Europe take place on Friday 10^th
   October at the Marriot, Grosvenor Square, London. For information visit
   [17]pakawards.org.uk
