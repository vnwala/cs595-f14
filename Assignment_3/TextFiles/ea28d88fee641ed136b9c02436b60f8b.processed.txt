Album Review

   The debut album from California-based duo Heffron Drive, 2014's Happy
   Mistakes showcases the group's melodic pop sound. Featuring the talents
   of Big Time Rush member Dustin Belt and touring guitarist Kendall
   Schmidt, Heffron Drive have been writing together since meeting in
   2008. They composed many of the songs on their debut during their
   off-time while on tour with BTR. This is earnest, yet infectiously
   radio-ready music that strikes a balance between sincere acoustic folk
   ("Parallel"), driving electronic dance music ("Nicotine"), and anthemic
   pop ("Panama"). [A Deluxe Edition added five bonus tracks.]

   rock- and dance music-influenced pop. Formed 2008, Heffron Drive
   features the talents of singer/guitarist and actor Kendall Schmidt and
   singer/guitarist and actor Dustin Belt. Originally from Kansas, Schmidt
   and Belt had previously played in a band together years earlier. It was
