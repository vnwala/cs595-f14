Home


   This site was created in loving memory of our daughter Anisette, and to
   bring awareness about bicornuate / septate uterus' and the effects it
   can have on your pregnancy.  Please feel free to look around, leave a
   comment with your story or check out the other helpful sites listed.
   My ultimate goal is to possibly change the way women, like myself, get
   a papsmear to where it includes a one time sonogram to check for a
   bicornuate or septate uterus.  You will find that in most cases, women
   find out that they have one of these uterine malformations when they
   are already pregnant and it is to late.  It seems like a long shot but
   it would definitely be worth it.
