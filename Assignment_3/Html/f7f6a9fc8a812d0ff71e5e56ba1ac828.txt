	<header class="entry-header clear">
					<div class="post-date"><a href="http://alifewellconsumed.com/2014/09/22/weekendrecap-2/" title="10:00 am" rel="bookmark"><time class="entry-date" datetime="September 22, 2014"><span class="entry-day">22</span><span class="entry-month">Sep</span><span class="entry-year">2014</span></time></a></div>				<h1 class="entry-title"><a href="http://alifewellconsumed.com/2014/09/22/weekendrecap-2/" rel="bookmark">Weekend Recap</a></h1>						<div class="entry-meta">
							<span class="cat-links">
			Posted in <a href="http://alifewellconsumed.com/category/misc/" rel="category tag">Misc.</a>, <a href="http://alifewellconsumed.com/category/weekend-recap/" rel="category tag">Weekend Recap</a>		</span>
			
	<span class="byline">
		by <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://alifewellconsumed.com/author/alifewellconsumed/" title="View all posts by alifewellconsumed" rel="author">alifewellconsumed</a></span>	</span>
				</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

		<div class="entry-content">
		<p>Happy Monday!</p>
<p>Our weekend was an uneventful one. Started out with dining out a Mexican joint <a href="http://topangacafe.ca">Topanga Cafe</a> on Friday night. It was delicious! Apparently it has been around since the 70s, which is amazing in itself.</p>
<div id="attachment_391" style="width: 310px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/img_6558.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-391" src="http://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/img_6558.jpg?w=300&#038;h=300" alt="Blended margaritas" width="300" height="300" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Blended margaritas</p></div>
<div id="attachment_392" style="width: 310px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/img_6557.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-392" src="http://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/img_6557.jpg?w=300&#038;h=300" alt="I had the flautas, Curtis had the burrito" width="300" height="300" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">I had the flautas, Curtis had the burrito</p></div>
<p>Later we watched <a href="http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/the_great_beauty/">The Great Beauty</a> an Italian film by Paulo Sorrentino. It was an excellent film and we highly recommend it. The rundown of it is an older gentleman, a former novelist, has just had his 65th birthday. He has been part of the social scene of Rome for decades and now is reflecting on his past and future. The dialogue between the characters and the scenery of Rome is what makes the film.<br />
<a href="https://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/800105_full.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-389" src="http://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/800105_full.jpg?w=211&#038;h=300" alt="MCDGRBE EC017"   /></a><br />
Saturday consisted of errands and taking the VW out for a drive. This was Piper&#8217;s highlight of his day.</p>
<div id="attachment_390" style="width: 310px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/img_6568.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-390" src="http://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/img_6568.jpg?w=300&#038;h=225" alt="Saturday's dinner" width="300" height="225" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Saturday&#8217;s dinner</p></div>
<p>We picked up ribs on the way home and had a rib charcuterie board. Ribs were spicy and fell off the bone. Afterwards we enjoyed watching the sci-fi flick <a href="http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/under_the_skin_2013/">Under the Skin</a> with ScarJo.<br />
<a href="https://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/film-under-the-skin-1.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-393" src="http://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/film-under-the-skin-1.jpg?w=300&#038;h=184" alt="film-under-the-skin-1" width="300" height="184" /></a><br />
Sunday I slept in and read in bed for a bit while sipping some tea. I really enjoy those mornings from time to time. With having a mind that is always racing with thoughts and ideas, I take advantage of those. Curtis was at work and I had the house to myself to do as I pleased. When he got home, we went out and ran an errand for my aunt then enjoyed a late lunch at <a href="www.paulsomelettery.com/">Paul&#8217;s Omelettery</a> with my sister. Paul&#8217;s is always a good idea, the food is decent, the prices reflect that and the staff are great!</p>
<div id="attachment_394" style="width: 235px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/img_6580.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-394" src="http://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/img_6580.jpg?w=225&#038;h=300" alt="inside Paul's" width="225" height="300" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Inside Paul&#8217;s</p></div>
<p>We took the VW out for might be the last time this year and stopped at the beach. It&#8217;s always sad to say goodbye to summer here in Vancouver. Yes we have it easier than up north and east of the Rockies, but I feel it is too short of a season. Another reason why I would like to move to Southern California.</p>
<p>As I&#8217;m typing this, I&#8217;m enjoying watching the F1 race from this morning in Singapore. This season was one of the more interesting ones the last few years. The rivalry between Hamilton and Rosberg is exciting and I am personally glad Vettel&#8217;s lack of skills are showing. Unfortunately this race, not much of one between Hamilton and Rosberg since Rosberg retired early due to mechanical issues.</p>
<div id="attachment_395" style="width: 310px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/afp-533643164.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-395" src="http://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/afp-533643164.jpg?w=300&#038;h=198" alt="Mohd Rasfan/AFP" width="300" height="198" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Mohd Rasfan/AFP</p></div>
<p>As I said, it was a pretty uneventful couple of days, but sometimes those are the best.</p>
<p>xo</p>

	<header class="entry-header clear">
					<div class="post-date"><a href="http://alifewellconsumed.com/2014/09/18/whatscookin-3/" title="1:00 pm" rel="bookmark"><time class="entry-date" datetime="September 18, 2014"><span class="entry-day">18</span><span class="entry-month">Sep</span><span class="entry-year">2014</span></time></a></div>				<h1 class="entry-title"><a href="http://alifewellconsumed.com/2014/09/18/whatscookin-3/" rel="bookmark">What&#8217;s Cookin&#8217;</a></h1>						<div class="entry-meta">
							<span class="cat-links">
			Posted in <a href="http://alifewellconsumed.com/category/recipes/" rel="category tag">Recipes</a>, <a href="http://alifewellconsumed.com/category/whats-cookin/" rel="category tag">What's Cookin'</a>		</span>
			
	<span class="byline">
		by <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://alifewellconsumed.com/author/alifewellconsumed/" title="View all posts by alifewellconsumed" rel="author">alifewellconsumed</a></span>	</span>
				</div><!-- .entry-meta -->
						</header><!-- .entry-header -->

		<div class="entry-content">
		<p>Hi all,</p>
<p>I appreciate recipes that are easy and flavourful that end up in your regular rotation for weeknight dinners. Out of my extensive cookbook collection, there is one person with many cookbooks that I turn to on a weekly basis for those recipes.</p>
<p>That author is <a href="http://www.donnahay.com.au">Donna Hay</a> from Australia.<br />
<a href="https://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/image-claremontquartercomau.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-354" src="http://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/image-claremontquartercomau.jpg?w=640" alt="image-claremontquartercomau"   /></a><br />
She is a food stylist, author and magazine editor. I love <a href="https://www.donnahay.com.au">Donna Hay</a>, from her great cookbooks, her magazine to her TV show. My love started for her with looking through my mom&#8217;s copies of her books. Now all of her cookbooks are in my collection, the photography is amazing and just that is reason to buy the books. I haven&#8217;t come across a recipe I haven&#8217;t enjoyed, from her <a href="http://www.amazon.ca/Fresh-Light-Donna-Hay/dp/1443424072/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1411014610&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=fresh+and+light">Fresh and Light</a>, <a href="http://www.amazon.ca/Seasons-Hay/dp/073229049X/ref=sr_1_1?s=books&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=undefined&amp;sr=1-1&amp;keywords=seasons+donna+hay">Seasons</a>, Modern Classics <a href="http://www.amazon.ca/Modern-Classics-Book-Donna-Hay/dp/0060095245/ref=pd_sim_b_1?ie=UTF8&amp;refRID=1QXRDN4FW4SMXZHCSSET">Vol. 1</a> &amp; <a href="http://www.amazon.ca/Modern-Classics-Book-Biscuits-Desserts/dp/0060525894/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=undefined&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=modern+classics+donna+hay">Vol. 2</a> and <a href="http://www.amazon.ca/Fast-Fresh-Simple-Donna-Hay/dp/1443407577/ref=sr_1_1?s=books&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1411014728&amp;sr=1-1&amp;keywords=fresh+and+simple">Fast, Fresh and Simple</a> books. Each issue of her magazine is inspiring, all staying seasonal. The photos trigger a saliva build up in my mouth, while I&#8217;m fantasizing about cooking each recipe. If you don&#8217;t own one of her books, I highly suggest doing so in the near future! You won&#8217;t regret it.</p>
<p>One of my favourites is the Lemon-Feta Chicken from her <a href="http://www.amazon.com/No-Time-Cook-Donna-Hay/dp/0732288169/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1411013252&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=no+time+to+cook">No Time to Cook</a> book.</p>
<p><a href="https://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/9780732288167.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-347" src="http://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/9780732288167.jpg?w=243&#038;h=300" alt="9780732288167" width="243" height="300" /></a></p>
<p><strong>Lemon-feta Chicken</strong><br />
serves 2</p>
<p>2 x 8oz chicken breast fillets, trimmed<br />
1 cup of feta, thickly sliced<br />
5 sprigs oregano<br />
1 tablespoon lemon zest<br />
2 tablespoons lemon juice<br />
olive oil, for sprinkling<br />
cracked black pepper<br />
green salad, optional</p>
<p>Preheat the oven to 180C (355F). Place chicken, feta, oregano, lemon zest and juice in a baking dish. Sprinkle with olive oil and pepper. Bake for 18 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through. Serve with a simple green salad, if desired.</p>
<p>Enjoy! xo</p>

							<span class="cat-links">
			Posted in <a href="http://alifewellconsumed.com/category/design-wednesday/" rel="category tag">Design Wednesday</a>		</span>
			
	<span class="byline">
		by <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://alifewellconsumed.com/author/alifewellconsumed/" title="View all posts by alifewellconsumed" rel="author">alifewellconsumed</a></span>	</span>

		<div class="entry-content">
		<p>Let&#8217;s talk about stationery this week. I love receiving business cards, especially when they are unique and memorable.</p>
<p>Stunning and memorable business cards being letterpress to die-cut could help beat off the competition. Business cards are the most essential tool of any professional in the world. Once you pass it to your potential client, there’s no receiving it back so when you take that one chance, make sure to leave a terrific impression and not see your business card tossed into the nearest trash.</p>
<p>Below are various examples of what can be accomplished.</p>
<p><strong>Letterpress</strong><br />
Letterpress printing has been around for hundreds of years. In this printing method, a surface with raised letters is inked and pressed to the surface of the printing substrate to reproduce an image in reverse.</p>
<div id="attachment_331" style="width: 310px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/bhrobinson.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-331" src="http://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/bhrobinson.jpg?w=300&#038;h=195" alt="Designed by Two Arms Inc." width="300" height="195" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Designed by Two Arms Inc.</p></div>
<div id="attachment_332" style="width: 310px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/letterpress_business_card_chair_02.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-332" src="http://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/letterpress_business_card_chair_02.jpg?w=300&#038;h=165" alt="Designed by Richard C. Evans" width="300" height="165" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Designed by Richard C. Evans</p></div>
<div id="attachment_333" style="width: 210px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/shyama.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-333" src="http://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/shyama.jpg?w=200&#038;h=300" alt="Designed by Shyama Golden " width="200" height="300" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Designed by Shyama Golden</p></div>
<p><strong>Die-Cut<br />
</strong>Die cut involves cutting irregular shapes in paper or paperboard using a die.<br />
<a href="https://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/creative-business-card-designs-for-inspiration1.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-336" src="http://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/creative-business-card-designs-for-inspiration1.jpg?w=175&#038;h=300" alt="Creative-Business-Card-Designs-for-Inspiration1" width="175" height="300" /></a></p>
<div id="attachment_337" style="width: 195px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/creative-business-card-designs-for-inspiration16.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-337" src="http://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/creative-business-card-designs-for-inspiration16.jpg?w=185&#038;h=300" alt="Designed by Marked for Trade"   /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Designed by Marked for Trade</p></div>
<div id="attachment_339" style="width: 210px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/creative-business-card-designs-for-inspiration2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-339" src="http://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/creative-business-card-designs-for-inspiration2.jpg?w=200&#038;h=300" alt="Designed by Chez Valois" width="200" height="300" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Designed by Chez Valois</p></div>
<p><strong>Using other materials<br />
</strong>Sometimes paper is just not enough to get your message across. Other materials used may be from wood, metal to rubber. Even baked goods as shown below!</p>
<div id="attachment_341" style="width: 248px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/creative-business-card-designs-for-inspiration26.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-341" src="http://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/creative-business-card-designs-for-inspiration26.jpg?w=238&#038;h=300" alt="Designed by Kapil Bhimekar" width="238" height="300" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Designed by Kapil Bhimekar</p></div>
<div id="attachment_342" style="width: 310px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/creative-business-card-designs-for-inspiration31.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-342" src="http://alifewellconsumed.files.wordpress.com/2014/09/creative-business-card-designs-for-inspiration31.jpg?w=300&#038;h=276" alt="Designed by Dizzy Designs" width="300" height="276" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Designed by Dizzy Designs</p></div>
<p>Are you inspired? I&#8217;m already dreaming up ideas..</p>
