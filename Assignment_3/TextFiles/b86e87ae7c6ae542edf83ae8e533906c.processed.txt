   Don't look at the book,

   look at the pages.

   Wishing I Was Seventeen
   by Tia Rose Mele
   The song ends and I hit repeat
   Again
   Because I miss you and those three words
   Help,
   but only a little.
   My screen lights up with
   A text.
   It’s my mother, she wants to talk about
   Wednesday,
   so I guess I’ll respond.
   That picture gets another like.
   The one
   Of you engulfed in smoke, working
   Magic,
   and I’m sad again.
   Twitter tells me what I know
   Gossip.
   This celeb couple split, this one got
   Together,
   but no photos of you.
   I try to focus on my mother
   Texting.
   She wants to know when I’m done
   With class
   and how much work I’ll have.
   She’s my mom and I can’t ignore
   Her.
   So I tell her I’m done at 5:30 and not to
   Worry,
   homework will get done.
   An ant crawls across my desk
   Carrying
   A crumb from the cookie I ate
   Earlier,
   he seems happy.
   You sing the last words again so I hit
   Repeat.
   My mom is satisfied by my
   Answers.
   I can’t see the ant anymore.

   Sometimes you reach your limit when it comes to writing about a certain
   subject, or a certain person.

   Lately I’ve found it really hard to write about Josh Hutcherson. Not
   because I don’t love him, I really do, but because I feel like I’ve
   written it all already and now I don’t know what else I can say.

   That being said, I’m going to be cutting BFF short. I’m going to give
   it a good proper ending, but it will be half as long as my original
   plan was. I just don’t think the quality is good enough to make up for
   the quantity. Plus, I have a job and school and life is catching up to
   me.

   I’m sorry if you’re disappointed (though I don’t know that many of you
   read BFF anyway). I hope if I take a break for a while, I’ll get my
   inspiration back.

   Ross Gets Dumped will still continue as planned, but I already wrote
   the outline of that one as 12 chapters so I’m not as worried about it.
   After that, I think I’ll stick to original work for a while. Like the
   one I’ll be posting on Wattpad in late October. I’m very excited about
   it.
