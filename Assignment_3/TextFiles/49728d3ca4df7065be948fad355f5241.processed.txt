[8]Faithful Promise

   [9]

   29 Monday Sep 2014

   Posted by [10]Kayla Stierwalt in [11]Poetry/Lyrics

   ≈ [12]Leave a comment

   Tags

   [13]Christian, [14]Christianity, [15]clouds, [16]deliverance,
   [17]dreamer, [18]dreams, [19]faith, [20]God, [21]hope, [22]life,
   [23]limits, [24]poem, [25]poetry, [26]promise, [27]promises, [28]silver
   lining, [29]sky, [30]truth

   I am a cloud

   Floating in the sky

   Infinity every direction beyond

   Except for the ground below


   I pray to land

   Upright on my feet

   The Son still shining through

   A way, truth and life in me


   Even when dark blankets

   Light still surrounds

   A silver lining so bright

   A seal of prosperous rain


   This life knows no limits

   When love abounds like endless sky

   Ever shifting beauty

   For a cloud by day and by night

[31]Eyes Closed

   [32]

   28 Sunday Sep 2014

   Posted by [33]Kayla Stierwalt in [34]Poetry/Lyrics

   ≈ [35]Leave a comment

   Tags

   [36]alone, [37]bear, [38]darkness, [39]depressed. sadness,
   [40]depression, [41]dreams, [42]hug, [43]poem, [44]poetry

   Withdrawing from the world

   I seek no friends, no talk

   Only my bear to squeeze

   No one else knows this blue

   Curtains drawn, night closes in

   The house gets dark again

   My heart dims as tears roll

   I sink right into my chair

   My bear in my lap sits

   As real as my old self

   Present in body solely

   Empty bodies, one full of fluff

   I crawl in bed alone

   For a time of intended darkness

   Eyes closed makes no difference

   When I can’t see waking

   I pull my bear close

   Drifting into another place

   In dreams I find myself

   Among others, alone only in theory

[46](Alone) In A One Man Army

   [47]

   27 Saturday Sep 2014

   Posted by [48]Kayla Stierwalt in [49]Poetry/Lyrics

   ≈ [50]Leave a comment

   Tags

   [51]alone, [52]depressed, [53]depression, [54]music, [55]poem,
   [56]poetry, [57]sadness, [58]solitude, [59]trapped

   Pushing away my last line

   I’m left truly alone

   Past my last defense

   It’s a one man army


   Attacked from the outside

   The beats are caged

   Not allowed to leave

   Only spill their remnants


   Triggered from outside

   The holes flow both ways

   Pain going in, emptiness prevails

   No permanent visitor but lonesome blues


   The melodies roll in

   The words a kind rhythm

   To pull me through

   Cleansing drops emerge through gray


   But when the songs end

   And I lay down my pen

   I sit on an empty couch

   In selected solitude, alone again

[78]What Tears May Come

   [79]

   18 Thursday Sep 2014

   Posted by [80]Kayla Stierwalt in [81]Poetry/Lyrics

   ≈ [82]Leave a comment

   Tags

   [83]crying, [84]depressed, [85]depression, [86]emptiness, [87]hope,
   [88]poem, [89]poetry, [90]sad, [91]sadness, [92]tears

   When crying becomes regular

   A sad song stirs tears

   The catharsis a reality


   Familiar sounds in a familiar place

   No longer provide comfort

   My eyes wash away normal


   Silent streams, heavy breaths

   Breathe in pain, exhale hope

   Mouth opens no words


   No action, just idea

   A picture far away inside

   My world I can’t touch


   I want to reach

   But my hands remain

   Stagnant at my side


   I get farther away

   Filling less of myself

   Faith holds my heart

[93]Turning, Spinning, Moving

   [94]

   17 Wednesday Sep 2014

   Posted by [95]Kayla Stierwalt in [96]Poetry/Lyrics

   ≈ [97]Leave a comment

   Tags

   [98]depressed, [99]depression, [100]heart, [101]light, [102]poem,
   [103]poetry, [104]sad, [105]sadness, [106]tears

   My heart rate rises

   And the tears return

   with nothing left to blame


   I’ve been washed clean

   The drugs gone for now

   With my head active


   The wheels turn again

   But so does the water wheel

   Words and water flowing


   Sources, destination unknown

   Head spinning in place

   My face lands in my hands


   What can I see

   Where can I point

   No aim with closed eyes


   Stay in place

   Move like clouds

   Light shining through

[107]Burn Marks

   [108]

   13 Saturday Sep 2014

   Posted by [109]Kayla Stierwalt in [110]Poetry/Lyrics

   ≈ [111]Leave a comment

   Tags

   [112]depression, [113]fire, [114]flame, [115]flames, [116]heart,

   My heart’s ablaze again

   I can’t seem to catch a break

   From the rapid burns

   Radiating through my chest


   Right at my center flames

   Rise to my surface

   Turning thoughts to ash

   One idea survives rubble


   I still pilfer through

   Finding the same despair

   Pieces of you from the whole

   Can this be contained


   Roots don’t fade

   This flame we feel

   It stays inside

   Right where it started


   Forever in spring’s bloom

[136]Melodic Remedy

   [137]

   11 Thursday Sep 2014

   Posted by [138]Kayla Stierwalt in [139]Poetry/Lyrics

   ≈ [140]Leave a comment

   Tags

   [141]company, [142]depression, [143]melancholy, [144]melody,

   They try to knock it

   The beauty in the pain

   That the music shares

   Better than I explain


   But I’m not the first

   To feel so low

   And won’t be the last

   To survive the blow


   So I listen widely

   To the company in melody

   A kindred melancholy

   Part of the remedy


   Then my words pour out

   An alternate perspective

   That I let go knowing

   My view has my directive

[151]Gripping Reminder

   [152]

   10 Wednesday Sep 2014

   Posted by [153]Kayla Stierwalt in [154]Poetry/Lyrics

   ≈ [155]Leave a comment

   Tags

   [156]alive, [157]depression, [158]drugs, [159]emotion, [160]enemy,
   [161]fog, [162]heart, [163]medication, [164]past, [165]poem,
   [166]poetry, [167]reminder, [168]restless, [169]sadness, [170]Satan,

   My heart’s in overdrive

   Spinning my head around

   A constant alarming reminder

   That to my past I’m bound


   As if I could forget

   I’m still alive

   The real me’s still inside

   For whom I continue to strive


   I can’t seem to tell

   Who is the enemy

   Is it Satan or the drugs

   Or is it simply me


   This cloud makes it hard

   Day and night are restless

   To a soul in fog

   Clinging emotional excess
