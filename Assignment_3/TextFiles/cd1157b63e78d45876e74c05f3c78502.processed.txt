   [12]I’m Stoked.

   One of my musical partners and best friends is an ocean apart from me
   right now, but somehow we’ve got the set list, the chord progressions,
   and the general feelings for each of the songs on our upcoming
   Christmas EP. We even have our duet for ‘Baby, It’s Cold Outside’
   started and are working on a handful of other pieces, all arranged by
   him, including a ridiculously sensual variation of ‘Santa Baby’ and a
   swing arrangement of ‘God Rest Ye Merry Gentlemen’. Stay tuned. It’s
   going to change your life.

                                                 -   [13]29 September 2014
