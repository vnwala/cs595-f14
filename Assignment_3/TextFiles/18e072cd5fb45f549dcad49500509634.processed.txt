[58]Nicki Minaj, Ariana Grande and Jessie J Score UK No.1 With 'Bang Bang'
       Posted by User
       Jessie J, Ariana Grande and Nicki Minaj have taken the number one
       spot in this week's UK singles chart with their song "Bang Bang".
       The single achieved 92,000 downloads and was streamed more than
       800,000 times to give an overall tally of almost 100,000 chart
       sales, according to data from the Official Charts Company.
       This achievement marks Jessie J's third number one single. The
       track also gives Nicki her first UK number one single and marks
       Ariana Grande's second after "Problem".
       Excited about the news, both Jessie and Nicki took to Instagram to
       show their appreciation.
       Jessie J: "Ladies we did it! THANK YOU for being so unbelievably
       amazing! For holding my hand on this part of my journey!" she
       wrote. "I love you both and am so happy for us to share this
       moment. Number one in the UK BABY!"
       Nicki Minaj: "Heard #BangBang is #1 in the UK. AAAAAHHHHHHHHH!
       Thank u guys! Can't wait to come see u!!!!! ItsMeJessieAndAri
       #HistoryInTheMaking mmmmuuuuaaahhhhh!"
