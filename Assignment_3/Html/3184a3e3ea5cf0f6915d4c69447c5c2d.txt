<p class="entry-meta"><time class="entry-time" itemprop="datePublished" datetime="2014-09-28T10:14:03+00:00">September 28, 2014</time> By <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person"><a href="http://www.robertlyon.org/author/Robert/" class="entry-author-link" itemprop="url" rel="author"><span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">Robert A Lyon</span></a></span> <span class="entry-comments-link"><a href="http://www.robertlyon.org/poetry/disorder-ethereal/#respond"><span class="dsq-postid" rel="445 http://www.robertlyon.org/?p=445">Leave a Comment</span></a></span> </p></header><div class="entry-content" itemprop="text"><figure id="attachment_447" style="width: 1200px;" class="wp-caption alignleft"><img class="size-full wp-image-447" src="http://www.robertlyon.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/disorderly-ethereal-souls.jpg" alt="Robert A Lyon, Robert Lyon, Rob Lyon, robertalyon, roblyon, robertlyon, robert_lyon_, poems, poetry, chaos, love, hate, disorder, smashing, robertlyon.org" width="1200" height="1663" data-wp-pid="447" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">fighting the. fire&lt;</figcaption></figure>

<p class="entry-meta"><time class="entry-time" itemprop="datePublished" datetime="2014-09-25T22:34:29+00:00">September 25, 2014</time> By <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person"><a href="http://www.robertlyon.org/author/Robert/" class="entry-author-link" itemprop="url" rel="author"><span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">Robert A Lyon</span></a></span> <span class="entry-comments-link"><a href="http://www.robertlyon.org/poetry/rise-sunset/#respond"><span class="dsq-postid" rel="433 http://www.robertlyon.org/?p=433">Leave a Comment</span></a></span> </p></header><div class="entry-content" itemprop="text"><figure id="attachment_437" style="width: 582px;" class="wp-caption alignleft"><img class="size-full wp-image-437" src="http://www.robertlyon.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/kiss-you-all-over.gif" alt="Robert A Lyon, Robert Lyon, Rob Lyon, Robert_Lyon_, Roblyon, RobertALyon, poems, poetry, robertlyon.org, love quotes, love poetry, love, love letters" width="582" height="582" data-wp-pid="437" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">My center is you.</figcaption></figure>
<p>There are times<br />
where <strong>I want to kiss you. all over</strong><br />
And <strong>hug you, forever.</strong><br />
I&#8217;d swim<br />
the deepest ocean<br />
brave the mightiest storms<br />
Even if it meant I&#8217;d <strong>find your heart</strong><br />
Deep down <strong>somewhere</strong><br />
near the bottom<br />
We&#8217;d only be able<br />
to rise<br />
from there.</p>
