<i>So, because I don&#39;t write that many reviews(I need to make a schedule), I figured it would be fun to throw some tags in the mix as well. This is the first one. I saw this floating around on some of the blogs I follow and I thought it would be fun to answer this questions.&#160;</i><br>

<b>
1. How many books are in your collection? </b><br>
<span lang="EN-GB">I do not have a serious count for this. I have an app on my phone which I used back in May to scan every single book I own. Or at least the ones I had in my house. There are also a bunch of them at my sisters and a few I left at my parents&#39; house when I moved out. And I figured that since I scanned them I bought about 20 books, which brings the total of books I own around 300 right now. I have actually banned myself from buying more at the moment, I need to actually read a bunch of the books I own.</span></div>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://girlonaspaceship.blogspot.nl/2013/08/review-artemis-fowl.html'>Review - Artemis Fowl</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-2120647944881630022' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
</div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<i>&#8220;Confidence is ignorance. If you&#39;re feeling cocky, it&#39;s because there&#39;s something you don&#39;t know.&#8221;&#160;</i></div>
<br>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-mG7BQYbZ-gg/UhoTH4CESwI/AAAAAAAAAVI/piXbvBsDsP8/s1600/Colfer%252C-Eoin---Artemis-Fowl.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-mG7BQYbZ-gg/UhoTH4CESwI/AAAAAAAAAVI/piXbvBsDsP8/s1600/Colfer%252C-Eoin---Artemis-Fowl.jpg"></a></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<br></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
&#9733;&#160;&#9733; &#9733; &#9733;&#160; </div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<a href="http://www.bookdepository.com/Artemis-Fowl-Eoin-Colfer/9781423124528/a_aid=pacely" target="_blank">The Book Depositor</a>y | <a href="http://www.goodreads.com/book/show/249747.Artemis_Fowl" target="_blank">GoodReads</a></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<br></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
I have waited writing this review until I finished the series. I did a few weeks ago, so now you will get a review.&#160; Friends of mine have told me to read it before and I figured why not. I devoured this first book(that is all broker and in pieces, but it looks amazing!) in a day. I loved it. I wanted more, but I was getting these books from my library and it took some time for all of them to arrive. The last one of the series I was able to borrow from a friend to read while on holiday.</div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
I think this is a series that every child should read. It is filled with action<i> </i>and humour. Give it to your kids, your nieces and your nephews. I am positive they will love the series.<br>There are 4 stars for this first book, but I rated these books from 2-4 stars. I would give the overal series somewhere between 3 and 4 stars.</div>

This book has been standing in my bookcase for 3 years before I finally picked it up to read it. The reason being the new Radio 4 audio play that came out this month. Why it was on the shelf for that long? I have no idea. I loved this book so much. I am actually a bit obsessed with it right now, I love it that much. It is described as a dark Alice in Wonderland type tale and I can only agree.<br>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://girlonaspaceship.blogspot.nl/2013/03/hello.html'>Hello</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-3826219315932388893' itemprop='description articleBody'>
My name is Rachel and I am currently 25 years old. English may not be my first language, but it is probably my favourite. I was born and raised in the Netherlands and started reading at a very young age. I love escaping in a world that a writer creates and just getting overwhelmed with feelings.<br />
I started reading in English when I was 11 years old and right now it is my favourite to read in. I cannot explain why, but somehow (for a non native speaker) things sound better in English. Things get lost in translation. I do enjoy reading books from Dutch writers in Dutch though. Otherwise I just prefer to read a book in English.<br />
<br />
I can't tell you what the future of this blog will be like, but I will just post my reviews here. You may or may not agree with me. That is okay. Please do leave a message in the comments for others to read, maybe you will inspire people to read. Brilliant.<br />
<br />
It is also possible that there will be some other posts on this blog. I also really enjoy watching movies and tv shows. I enjoy cooking, but I am terrible at that. And since a month or so, I also really enjoy knitting. And finally, I love photography and DIY. So who knows, maybe you will also see some updates about the projects I have going on.<br />
<br />
I hope you will stop by my blog every now and then and give me some feedback about how I'm doing.<br />
