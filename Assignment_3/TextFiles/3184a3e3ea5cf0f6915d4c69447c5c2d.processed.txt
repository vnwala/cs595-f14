[22]Rise to a Sunset

   September 25, 2014 By [23]Robert A Lyon [24]Leave a Comment
   Robert A Lyon, Robert Lyon, Rob Lyon, Robert_Lyon_, Roblyon,
   RobertALyon, poems, poetry, robertlyon.org, love quotes, love poetry,
   love, love letters My center is you.

   There are times
   where I want to kiss you. all over
   And hug you, forever.
   I’d swim
   the deepest ocean
   brave the mightiest storms
   Even if it meant I’d find your heart
   Deep down somewhere
   near the bottom
   We’d only be able
   to rise
   from there.

[40]Always Elusive

   September 24, 2014 By [41]Robert A Lyon [42]Leave a Comment
   [43]Robert A Lyon, Robert Lyon, Rob Lyon,
   Robert_Lyon_,RobertALyon,roblyon,robertlyon.org,poems,poetry,love,love
   letters When I cower inside myself I fall to you.

   I need more time
   to get me out
   all the things
   that scurry about.
   Hidden away
   I don’t know where
   Who it is elusive up there
   So let me say
   This much is true
   when I’m all alone ,
   my thoughts scream
   You

   – Robert A Lyon
