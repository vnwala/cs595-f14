<h5>Amid Amidi (<a onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/twitter.com/amid');"  href="http://twitter.com/amid">@amid</A>/<a onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/www.linkedin.com/in/amidamidi/');"  href="http://www.linkedin.com/in/amidamidi/">LinkedIn</A>) is an author, historian, and consultant.</h5>
<p>I&#8217;m the official biographer of Oscar-winning animation director and jazz bandleader Ward Kimball, and the youngest recipient of the <a onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/www.tla-online.org/awards/bookawards/wall-award-winners/');"  href="http://www.tla-online.org/awards/bookawards/wall-award-winners/">Theatre Library Association Award</A> which I received for the book <a onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/www.amazon.com/gp/product/0811847314/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_tl?ie=UTF8&#038;camp=1789&#038;creative=9325&#038;creativeASIN=0811847314&#038;linkCode=as2&#038;tag=animationblast08');"  href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0811847314/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_tl?ie=UTF8&#038;camp=1789&#038;creative=9325&#038;creativeASIN=0811847314&#038;linkCode=as2&#038;tag=animationblast08"><em>Cartoon Modern: Style and Design in 1950s Animation</em></a><img src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=animationblast08&#038;l=as2&#038;o=1&#038;a=0811847314" width="1" height="1" border="0" alt="" style="border:none !important; margin:0px !important;" /> (2006, Chronicle Books). </p>
<p>I&#8217;ve written <em>The Art of Pixar: The Complete Color Scripts and Select Art from 25 Years of Animation</em> for Pixar; <I><a onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/charette.org/book-design-close-up-art-of-robots/');"  href="http://charette.org/book-design-close-up-art-of-robots/">The Art of Robots</A></I> for 20th Century Fox about the making of the Fox/Blue Sky computer animated feature <I>Robots;</I> <em>A Sketchy Past: The Art of Peter de Sève</em>; and edited and designed the book <em>Inside UPA,</em> a compilation of unpublished photographs from the legendary UPA Animation Studios.</p>
<p>I&#8217;ve worked in the animation industry in a number of capacities. For four years, I worked for the animation company <strong>Spümcø</strong>, where I served as the production manager of Spumco-LA during the production of <em>Ren &#038; Stimpy: Adult Party Cartoon</em> (2002-03). Between 1998 and 2006, I published nine print issues of <em><strong>Animation Blast</strong>,</em> a publication that featured interviews and biographies of numerous animation legends, including Iwao Takamoto, Gene Hazelton, Pete Alvarado, Ed Benedict, Tom Oreb, Jim Tyer and John Dunn. In 1998, <I><strong>Animation World Magazine</strong></I> hired me to become the associate editor of their publication, which was the first monthly electronic magazine devoted to animation. On multiple occasions, I have served as an expert witness on character and production design for <strong>DreamWorks Animation</strong>.</p>
<p>Cartoon Brew was launched in March 2004 in partnership with Jerry Beck. In September 2008, we launched <A HREF="http://www.cartoonbrew.com/brewtv/">Cartoon Brew TV</A>, an exclusive on-line showcase for the world&#8217;s finest contemporary animated shorts. I became sole owner of Cartoon Brew in February 2013.</p>
