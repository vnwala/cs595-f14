              <h2 class="block-title"><span>About The Teamsters</span></h2>
        
      <p>Founded in 1903, the Teamsters mission is to organize and educate workers towards a higher standard of living.</p>
<p>There are currently 1.4 million members under 21 Industrial Divisions that include virtually every occupation imaginable, both professional and non professional, private sector and public sector.</p>
