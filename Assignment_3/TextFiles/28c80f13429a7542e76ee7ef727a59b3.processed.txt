About Theneeds

   Theneeds is the best place to discover news, articles, videos, social
   posts, and more, tailored to your interests. In a nutshell, Theneeds
   scours the web so you don't have to, constantly learning about your
   interests, and bringing you only the web you love.
   Theneeds pulls from thousands of sources, from top sites to cool blogs,
   social networks, and people just like you, and its advanced AI
   technology constantly learns from your activity to get smarter and more
   relevant the more you use it. Enjoy the best content from The New York
   Times, CNN, Huffington Post, YouTube, Vogue, Rolling Stone, and
   thousands more, in one single place.
