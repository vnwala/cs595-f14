                <div class="caption"><p><em><strong>Echo</strong></em></p>
<p>Echo is a temporary public sculpture installation I just finished for my kinetic sculpture class. I titled it so because if you stand in the center of the formation of stones and speak, you literally hear your voice echoed back to you off of the circular concrete bench surrounding the piece. Additionally, the stones are &#8220;charged&#8221; by the sun and thus provide a soft metaphorical echo of the sun&#8217;s light.</p>
<p>2014</p></div>
