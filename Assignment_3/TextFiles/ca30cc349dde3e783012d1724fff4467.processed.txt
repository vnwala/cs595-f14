   Tha Joker follows up the dope 2013 mixtape The Explanation with his
   first release of 2014, Meanwhile. Featuring the hot "Vegas Nights",
   "Run It" and the banger "Slight Work" Meanwhile displays Tha Joker in
   rarified form. Meanwhile feature guest spots fromDizzy Wright,
   Starlito, Rich The Kid and Don Trip and production from Big
   Fruit,DoughBoyBeats, Tekneek, and more.
