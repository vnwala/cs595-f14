				<span class="sep">Posted on </span><a href="http://yourwowmoney.com/post-show-news/" title="3:16 PM" rel="bookmark"><time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-09-14T15:16:17+00:00">September 14, 2014</time></a><span class="by-author"> <span class="sep"> by </span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://yourwowmoney.com/author/lady-havah/" title="View all posts by Lady Havah" rel="author">Lady Havah</a></span></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

				<div class="entry-content">
			<p>It is with great excitement that we announce the return of the undermine journal! Kero found the note below after the creation of this week’s show and we want to make sure that you had the news!</p>
<p>Welcome back TUJ!! I missed you.</p>
<p>~~LadyHavah</p>
<p>from <a href="https://theunderminejournal.com/#earthen-ring/horde">https://theunderminejournal.com/#earthen-ring/horde</a></p>
<p><b>September 3, 2014</b></p>
<p>We&#8217;ve recently had to move our operation to a new facility, and our old equipment and data could not come with us. So, what&#8217;s old is new again, and we&#8217;re back in <b>beta testing</b> as things get back up to speed.</p>
<p>95% of the TUJ code is newly written, and the underlying system is a fresh install. This means that many of the features we once shared are no longer available, but we tried to get the most important stuff back up and running. Items, sellers, and battle pets pages can all be found behind the Search box in the top right corner.</p>
<p>Our data collection is fresh as well, and we no longer have the old history data. This isn&#8217;t as big a problem as you&#8217;d guess, since realms are getting connected all the time, and the Alliance and Horde auction houses will be joined with WoW 6.0 later this season anyway. When that happens, we&#8217;ll wipe the data once again, since it&#8217;ll be a whole new market.</p>
<p>Category pages are not yet available, but we plan on getting those back up and running over the next few weeks. There are no plans to rebuild market notifications, the market API, or the addon. EU realms are also unavailable, though we plan on supporting all EU realms when the Warlords of Draenor pre-patch is released.</p>
<p>Since we are beta testing, we welcome your feedback <a href="http://stormspire.net/official-forum-undermine-journal/">in our official forum</a>. Expect things to change and grow over the coming weeks.</p>
<p><strong>Thank you for your patience and support while we were away. We hope you like our new look, and we&#8217;re happy to continue serving your auction house data needs.</strong></p>
