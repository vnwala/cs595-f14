		<div class="post-entry">
			<p>I am a cloud</p>
<p>Floating in the sky</p>
<p>Infinity every direction beyond</p>
<p>Except for the ground below</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>I pray to land</p>
<p>Upright on my feet</p>
<p>The Son still shining through</p>
<p>A way, truth and life in me</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Even when dark blankets</p>
<p>Light still surrounds</p>
<p>A silver lining so bright</p>
<p>A seal of prosperous rain</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>This life knows no limits</p>
<p>When love abounds like endless sky</p>
<p>Ever shifting beauty</p>
<p>For a cloud by day and by night</p>

		<div class="post-entry">
			<p>Withdrawing from the world</p>
<p>I seek no friends, no talk</p>
<p>Only my bear to squeeze</p>
<p>No one else knows this blue</p>
<p>Curtains drawn, night closes in</p>
<p>The house gets dark again</p>
<p>My heart dims as tears roll</p>
<p>I sink right into my chair</p>
<p>My bear in my lap sits</p>
<p>As real as my old self</p>
<p>Present in body solely</p>
<p>Empty bodies, one full of fluff</p>
<p>I crawl in bed alone</p>
<p>For a time of intended darkness</p>
<p>Eyes closed makes no difference</p>
<p>When I can’t see waking</p>
<p>I pull my bear close</p>
<p>Drifting into another place</p>
<p>In dreams I find myself</p>
<p>Among others, alone only in theory</p>

		<div class="post-entry">
			<p>Pushing away my last line</p>
<p>I’m left truly alone</p>
<p>Past my last defense</p>
<p>It’s a one man army</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Attacked from the outside</p>
<p>The beats are caged</p>
<p>Not allowed to leave</p>
<p>Only spill their remnants</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Triggered from outside</p>
<p>The holes flow both ways</p>
<p>Pain going in, emptiness prevails</p>
<p>No permanent visitor but lonesome blues</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The melodies roll in</p>
<p>The words a kind rhythm</p>
<p>To pull me through</p>
<p>Cleansing drops emerge through gray</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>But when the songs end</p>
<p>And I lay down my pen</p>
<p>I sit on an empty couch</p>
<p>In selected solitude, alone again</p>

			<p>When crying becomes regular</p>
<p>A sad song stirs tears</p>
<p>The catharsis a reality</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Familiar sounds in a familiar place</p>
<p>No longer provide comfort</p>
<p>My eyes wash away normal</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Silent streams, heavy breaths</p>
<p>Breathe in pain, exhale hope</p>
<p>Mouth opens no words</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>But my hands remain</p>
<p>Stagnant at my side</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>I get farther away</p>
<p>Filling less of myself</p>
<p>Faith holds my heart</p>
