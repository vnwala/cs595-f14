<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
If you haven't figured already, I'm Sherlock MAD!</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
And after uploading <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w7KcFBHBKL8" target="_blank">my first Youtube video</a> just under 3 weeks ago,</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
I thought, why not? Why not upload a few more Youtube videos here and there?</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
Hence, the new and improved BBC Sherlock Tag was born! </div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /><object class="BLOGGER-youtube-video" classid="clsid:D27CDB6E-AE6D-11cf-96B8-444553540000" codebase="http://download.macromedia.com/pub/shockwave/cabs/flash/swflash.cab#version=6,0,40,0" data-thumbnail-src="https://i.ytimg.com/vi/xHLUxJvEASc/0.jpg" height="300" width="500"><param name="movie" value="https://www.youtube.com/v/xHLUxJvEASc?version=3&f=user_uploads&c=google-webdrive-0&app=youtube_gdata" /><param name="bgcolor" value="#FFFFFF" /><param name="allowFullScreen" value="true" /><embed width="500" height="300"  src="https://www.youtube.com/v/xHLUxJvEASc?version=3&f=user_uploads&c=google-webdrive-0&app=youtube_gdata" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" allowfullscreen="true"></embed></object></div>
<br />
I tagged a few people to get the ball rolling but if you fancy doing the tag yourself, here are the questions for you:<br /><br />1. Spell Your Name with Sherlock characters or related words.<br />2. Name your favourite Sherlock moment to date.<br />3. The character you are most like.<br />4. If Sherlock characters had a ordinary jobs<br />5. Your best impression of a character.<br />6. If you were a murder case for Sherlock, how would it be done?<br />7. Describe Mofftiss in one word.<br />8. Draw your favourite ship.<br />9. Your craziest predictions for the next series.<br />10. Is the next series of Sherlock out yet?<br />
<br />
<div style="text-align: center;">
Trust me, you'll have A LOT of fun answering them!</div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<br /></div>

<b>World War One Centenary</b><br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
The beginning of this week marked 100 years since World War One. A few weeks ago, I blogged about my sister performing in a choir commemorating the centenary of World War One and the beginning of this week was the day that the world stood still to remember.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
&nbsp;From 10pm to 11pm on Monday, people in the UK turned off all their lights but one to remember the 17 million of the fallen during World War One.</div>
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<object class="BLOGGER-youtube-video" classid="clsid:D27CDB6E-AE6D-11cf-96B8-444553540000" codebase="http://download.macromedia.com/pub/shockwave/cabs/flash/swflash.cab#version=6,0,40,0" data-thumbnail-src="https://ytimg.googleusercontent.com/vi/ZP5IPzh1OEE/0.jpg" height="266" width="320"><param name="movie" value="https://youtube.googleapis.com/v/ZP5IPzh1OEE&amp;source=uds"><param name="bgcolor" value="#FFFFFF"><param name="allowFullScreen" value="true"><embed width="320" height="266" src="https://youtube.googleapis.com/v/ZP5IPzh1OEE&amp;source=uds" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" allowfullscreen="true"></object></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
(<a href="http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-28650525" target="_blank">BBC One News coverage</a>) </div>
<br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
A very beautiful gesture that the Trafford Centre organised in salutation was the Million Poppy Cascade. This is where they let poppies fall to symbolise those who fell during the World War.</div>
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<object class="BLOGGER-youtube-video" classid="clsid:D27CDB6E-AE6D-11cf-96B8-444553540000" codebase="http://download.macromedia.com/pub/shockwave/cabs/flash/swflash.cab#version=6,0,40,0" data-thumbnail-src="https://ytimg.googleusercontent.com/vi/Gm9VpuuLGvA/0.jpg" height="266" width="320"><param name="movie" value="https://youtube.googleapis.com/v/Gm9VpuuLGvA&amp;source=uds"><param name="bgcolor" value="#FFFFFF"><param name="allowFullScreen" value="true"><embed width="320" height="266" src="https://youtube.googleapis.com/v/Gm9VpuuLGvA&amp;source=uds" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" allowfullscreen="true"></object></div>
<br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
It's times like this when you begin to reflect upon the world you live in and are a part of. These soldiers and civilians put their own lives on the line for a better world for the future. Can we even start to imagine the bravery? It makes you think, would you do the same?</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Are you someone who would sacrifice your own needs for another? And when I ask these questions, I don't only mean would you go to war to stand for something you believe in but also for the smaller things in life. Like when you see someone's car has broken down on the road, do you stop and help? Or when someone is teasing somebody else in a way that makes them uncomfortable, are you the type of person who would step in? Or when you see someone pickpocket someone but they are still unaware of it, would you try and stop them?</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
I'm no saint and I put my hands up, there have been times where I've stand and just watched things unravel. I guess it's the fear that you don't want to get involved that wins over the desire to help. But in a world where war and violence still exists strongly, not lending a small helping hand in everyday life seems petty in comparison.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
There's a saying by Albert Einstein, <span style="font-size: small;"><i><b>"Be a voice, not an Echo."</b></i></span>.</div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Make a stand in one what you believe in, even if you are the only one but I think that being an echo is still just as powerful. By being the echo, you constantly repeat what you preach, like how nothing should stop us from standing up for what we believe in; like how nothing should stop us from offering a helping hand.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
If you were in their situation and asked for help, surely you'd hope that you would be someone who would.</div>
<br />
<div style="text-align: center;">

<b>Capturing my life in snaps.&nbsp;</b></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<b><br /></b></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<i>I am by no means a henna/mehndi artiste professional but I do try!</i></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<i>This is all from years of practice as growing up, I had nobody else to do my own mehndi but myself. </i></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">

<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<i>My little sister's mehndi with her red and gold polish to match her Eid clothes.</i></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-AQDiqNXWmzo/U-PwU6ZzHzI/AAAAAAAAAcI/aGICX_jZ3Oo/s1600/mehndi+2.jpg" height="320" width="239" /></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
&nbsp;<i>My mum hardly ever wants to get her mehndi done and the one occasion that she allows me to, the mehndi tube clogs up half way through hence the small design!</i></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">

<div style="text-align: center;">
<i>And finally, my own hand.<br />I'm quite fond of the one-sided style.</i></div>

<b>Eid Mubarak</b><br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
The
 beginning of this week marked Eid-ul-Fitr which is a celebratory day at
 the end of the month of fasting; Ramadan. It's a day full of food, 
prayer, family (not necessarily in that order!) and most importantly 
gratitude.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-qVjytDd_hA0/U-UEbcYNkuI/AAAAAAAAAeU/FKtOAmSPy1U/s1600/Capture+2.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-qVjytDd_hA0/U-UEbcYNkuI/AAAAAAAAAeU/FKtOAmSPy1U/s1600/Capture+2.JPG" height="213" width="320" /></a></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
The day usually starts of with Eid prayer at the mosque. If 
not already paid prior to Eid, Fitrana (also known as Zakat-ul-Fitr) is 
paid. This is a certain amount of money paid per person per household to
 the mosque. The amount can vary as it is based on how much 2.5kg of 
wheat costs. For example, a few years ago it was &#163;2.50 and this year 
it's &#163;5 per person.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
The Fitrana money is then given and 
used by less fortunate Muslims to pay for any needs they may have for 
Eid. As Eid is a day of celebration for all Muslims, irrespective of 
wealth, therefore it is our duty to help others to celebrate it with us.
 For example, the money could help pay for a filling, Eid meal for a 
family or buy new clothes for them to wear.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
After Eid 
prayers, the family gathers together to eat a banquet of food! And 
funnily enough, it's easy to think that now you can eat during the day 
that someone will have to pin you down to stop, but often you fill up 
quite quickly as your body is still used to the month of fasting.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Though physical presents can be given, money is often gifted from the elderly to the young (<i>Eidi).</i>So
 if you're a young'un with A LOT of elder relatives, be ready to collect
 millions as you run to the corner shop to buy yourself some millions!</div>
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-3KyJhfaMPao/U-UDS_f4YvI/AAAAAAAAAeI/ayulBoGNo18/s1600/Capture.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-3KyJhfaMPao/U-UDS_f4YvI/AAAAAAAAAeI/ayulBoGNo18/s1600/Capture.JPG" height="245" width="320" /></a></div>
<br />
<div style="text-align: center;">
As
 with anything, the novelty of Eid may start to rub off as you get older
 but either way, it's such a meaningful day where family come together, 
eat food and be merry!</div>

<span style="font-family: inherit;"><b>Benedict Cumberbirthday with Madame Tussaud</b></span><br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://25.media.tumblr.com/87c26b9fd9d65cd00087ce6b1dd4a218/tumblr_mg85691t421qzgltlo1_500.gif" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://25.media.tumblr.com/87c26b9fd9d65cd00087ce6b1dd4a218/tumblr_mg85691t421qzgltlo1_500.gif" height="192" width="320" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://24.media.tumblr.com/5ff1d7ec6ff049aeeaa3394f3d7ed5b7/tumblr_mg85691t421qzgltlo2_500.gif" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://24.media.tumblr.com/5ff1d7ec6ff049aeeaa3394f3d7ed5b7/tumblr_mg85691t421qzgltlo2_500.gif" height="192" width="320" /></a></div>
<span style="font-family: inherit;">So it was the Batch's birthday last week but as my Weekly Thought Catcher post last week was all about my Graduation, there was no room for the Batch apart from Cumberbatch the Cardboard Cut Out! (<a href="http://skeenakeepsmiling.blogspot.co.uk/2014/07/i-did-thing-graduation.html" target="_blank">Read more about it here!</a>)</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">You may wonder why I'm bothered that it was Cumberbatch's birthday, and though it was very hard to ignore with #HappyBirthdayBenedictCumbebatch trending on Twitter and my whole timeline being full with nothing but Birthday Batch, it was what the Cumbercookies did collectively that I want to congratulate.</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">There were many charitable initiatives that the Cumberbatch fans launched in celebration of Birthday Batch. From a Batch of Kindness which was simply doing a good deed for the day and spreading extra smiles to charity fundraising. One of the charities that fans fundraised for was the Prince's Trust which is a charity that Benedict is an ambassador for. The <a href="http://www.princes-trust.org.uk/" target="_blank">Prince's Trust</a> is a charity that helps disadvantaged young people in the UK to help and support them to get into education and work. Currently the Birthday Batch collections has raised over &#163;15,500 for the Prince's Trust which is amazing! Despite the negative stereotype of fans being overly obsessive, I love how so many people from all over the world can raise so much money in the name of one man: Benedict Cumberbatch.</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">I believe the fundraising page is still open for donations. So if you'd like to get involved and donate to the Prince's Trust you can do by clicking <a href="https://www.justgiving.com/cumberbatchwebpt/" target="_blank">here.</a></span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<a href="http://www.madametussauds.com/SiteImages/Assets/1/Benedict_Cumberbatch_Sitting_Madame_Tussauds_London_Tall.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img alt="" border="0" class="leadImage" src="http://www.madametussauds.com/SiteImages/Assets/1/Benedict_Cumberbatch_Sitting_Madame_Tussauds_London_Tall.jpg" height="320" width="225" /></a><span style="font-family: inherit;">Also sneaking in some&nbsp;newer news, the Batch will finally be able to photobomb himself by means of his own Madame Tussaud waxwork! I personally have never been to Madame Tussaud's but would really love to wander round one day if I get the chance to. Maybe I can upgrade my picture with Cumberbatch the Cardboard Cut Out to Cumberbatch the Waxwork!</span><br />
<br />
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><a href="http://www.madametussauds.com/London/NewsAndEvents/BenedictAnnounce.aspx" target="_blank">Read the official Madame Tussauds announcement on their website here!</a>&nbsp;</span></div>

<span style="font-family: inherit;"><b>Baby Royal Birthday!</b></span><br />
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">Can you believe that Baby Prince George is already 1 years old?!<b> </b></span></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><img alt="As he turns 1, it is becoming more and more apparent that George has a real eye for nature." class="bf_dom" src="http://s3-ec.buzzfed.com/static/2014-07/22/3/enhanced/webdr02/enhanced-buzz-29152-1406012624-18.jpg" height="244" width="320" />&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">(Image released for Prince George's birthday by John Stillwell) </span></div>
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><br /></span>
<br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">Who remembers the hysteria that this little chubby brought upon his arrival? It was all over the news worldwide and on every single channel that you flicked on. There were running updates all over the place and was pretty hard to miss even with your eyes and ears closed!</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">As exciting as it was to know that we'd be having a new little edition to the Royal Family, I did feel that the media coverage got a little too overcrowded. There were reporters and media camping right outside the hospital for days wanting to get the first glance of the baby prince. I understand it's something everybody wants to be the first to catch and that it's their job to report such a momentous moment but I did feel sorry for them, especially Kate, with all this attention. She looked great having just had a baby, but I imagine when you've just had a baby, the very last thing you want is the whole world watch you and take pictures of you as you leave the hospital. I definitely admire Kate for still being courteous, despite having just delivered a baby!</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">There are a few people who are skeptical of the whole Royal Family, and I definitely have my opinions on certain members of the family but I really do love the little family unit that is Kate, Will and George. There's just something about them being young and just genuinely nice people, that makes them so loveable. And quite honestly, Kate really reminds me of Princess Diana (whom my mum has always admired - to the extent of collecting books on her - and has rubbed off me!) and as sad as it is that Princess Diana isn't here to see her eldest son, daughter-in-law and grandson, I'm sure she would be so proud!</span></div>

          <div class="date-posts">
        
<div class='post-outer'>
<div class='post hentry' itemprop='blogPost' itemscope='itemscope' itemtype='http://schema.org/BlogPosting'>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://skeenakeepsmiling.blogspot.co.uk/2014/07/i-did-thing-graduation.html'>I did a thing: GRADUATION!</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'><div class='post-share-buttons goog-inline-block'>
<a class='goog-inline-block share-button sb-email' href='http://www.blogger.com/share-post.g?blogID=6096219630196850848&postID=2956629837526529227&target=email' target='_blank' title='Email This'><span class='share-button-link-text'>Email This</span></a><a class='goog-inline-block share-button sb-blog' href='http://www.blogger.com/share-post.g?blogID=6096219630196850848&postID=2956629837526529227&target=blog' onclick='window.open(this.href, "_blank", "height=270,width=475"); return false;' target='_blank' title='BlogThis!'><span class='share-button-link-text'>BlogThis!</span></a><a class='goog-inline-block share-button sb-twitter' href='http://www.blogger.com/share-post.g?blogID=6096219630196850848&postID=2956629837526529227&target=twitter' target='_blank' title='Share to Twitter'><span class='share-button-link-text'>Share to Twitter</span></a><a class='goog-inline-block share-button sb-facebook' href='http://www.blogger.com/share-post.g?blogID=6096219630196850848&postID=2956629837526529227&target=facebook' onclick='window.open(this.href, "_blank", "height=430,width=640"); return false;' target='_blank' title='Share to Facebook'><span class='share-button-link-text'>Share to Facebook</span></a><a class='goog-inline-block share-button sb-pinterest' href='http://www.blogger.com/share-post.g?blogID=6096219630196850848&postID=2956629837526529227&target=pinterest' target='_blank' title='Share to Pinterest'><span class='share-button-link-text'>Share to Pinterest</span></a><div class='goog-inline-block dummy-container'><g:plusone source='blogger:blog:plusone' href='http://skeenakeepsmiling.blogspot.com/2014/07/i-did-thing-graduation.html' size='medium' width='300' annotation='inline'/></div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-2956629837526529227' itemprop='articleBody'>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-CaHuCjrUfPE/U96qy6wizQI/AAAAAAAAAaM/xb0Aw5sGI-o/s1600/graduation.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em; text-align: center;"><img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-CaHuCjrUfPE/U96qy6wizQI/AAAAAAAAAaM/xb0Aw5sGI-o/s1600/graduation.jpg" height="155" width="400" /></a></span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">After being sent that in an email near the end of my final semester by my University, I actually did it. I made it. And it is the most surreal, unexplainable, indescribable feeling that when it happens it's like you're just wandering around in a dream.</span></div>
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><b>Graduation Outfit</b></span> <br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">So after my little panic at the end of my <a href="http://skeenakeepsmiling.blogspot.co.uk/2014/07/school-sherlock-and-style.html" target="_blank">School, Sherlock and Style post</a> two weeks ago, I dug into my smart clothes basket and decided to wear this green,&nbsp;skater dress which has an indented print in the material along with a pair of black, smart trousers and a matching plain, green scarf.</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">I was all set but then two days before my ceremony, my mum walks into my room and says "Right, let's go and buy you some Graduation clothes." and off I was whisked away!</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-7DPjh-88LHE/U-P1T5EzF9I/AAAAAAAAAcs/jweBFr1ZTmk/s1600/graduation+outfit.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: right; float: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-7DPjh-88LHE/U-P1T5EzF9I/AAAAAAAAAcs/jweBFr1ZTmk/s1600/graduation+outfit.jpg" height="320" width="239" /></a><span style="font-family: inherit;">I didn't want my mum to spend much, if anything at all, but we ended up getting this knee-length, sleeveless, navy blue dress which had a black strip around the bottom with a pink belt accessory. We also a white, chiffon blouse to go underneath and a pair of chino-cut, smart, black trousers with a bold hem down the front. And actually, the blouse was several sizes too big but as it was on sale, it was the last one. But it all worked out because as it was being worn under the dress, all that could be seen was the sleeves anyways.</span><br />
<br />
<span style="font-family: inherit;">I paired this new graduation outfit with my own pink scarf to match the pink belt that came with the dress and a pair of blue wedged heels that I bought from my local market months ago but had never found an occasion to wear them. But man, my feet were pleading mercy by the end of the day!</span></div>
<div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><b>Graduation Ceremony</b></span><br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">Now the ceremony was scheduled for an hour-ish. We were advised to arrive at least 90mins before as even though there were tickets allocated for guests, the seats were first come first served. So we arrived at 10:30am and my ceremony started at 12:15pm. I got dressed in my graduation cap and gown, took some pictures with the professional photographer, took a picture with Cumberbatch the Cut Out (I shall explain later!) and then to the graduation ceremony queue it was.</span></div>
</div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><br /></span></div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><b>Graduation Etiquette</b></span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">As we entered the hall, we were told to take off our caps as the majority of us were already wearing them. We sat in our seats and waited. A speech was made and the guest were welcomed as lecturers and academics processed to sit on the stage. The graduands all sat on the left and all the guests say on the right of the Hall.</span><br />
</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">After speeches were made,</span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-X2s_kxUr67A/U-P2WIglv1I/AAAAAAAAAc4/96jIbA0bN64/s1600/graduation+meme.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-X2s_kxUr67A/U-P2WIglv1I/AAAAAAAAAc4/96jIbA0bN64/s1600/graduation+meme.JPG" height="282" width="320" /></a></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">&nbsp;the calling of graduates' names began.</span></div>
<br />
<span style="font-family: inherit;">As names were being called out, students queued up by the side of the stage where two graduation gown staff were poised ready to fix up your misbehaving gown ready for your big moment. As you reach the front of the line and the stairs to the stage, your name is read out by the President or Dean of your faculty. You walk on stage with your graduation cap in your left hand and shake the head of your faculty's hand with your right. You walk down the steps at the centre of the stage on the left and are handed your graduation certificate where you then return to your seat. And just like that, that moment is over in a flash.</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">After all graduands have returned back to their seats with their certificates, a few more words of congratulations is spoken on stage. You think it's all over until all graduands are asked to stand. We do and then we are congratulated once more as we are asked to put on our graduation caps with the tassels on the left. After a few moments of fiddling, the hall erupts with applause and shouts and whistles.</span><br />
</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<div style="text-align: center;">
<i><b><span style="font-family: inherit;">We did it. Class of 2014.</span></b></i></div>
<span style="font-family: inherit;"></span><br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-LCs-Rooa-gs/U-P2legeybI/AAAAAAAAAdo/ln6gfLgNttQ/s1600/graduation+pup.png" height="320" width="230" /></div>
</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">The organ plays and we follow out in the procession.</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">Now Cumberbatch the Cut Out. He, along with other well known names such as Professor Brian Cox, Alan Turing, Jack Whitehall and Emily Pankhurst, are alumni to my university which means that they have studied there. And a fun little thing that the university did was have cardboard cut outs of them, amongst other famous alumni, on campus. So naturally, the batch and I picture needed to happen.</span><br />
<br />
<span style="font-family: inherit;">Also, there was a photo booth by the cut outs with silly props which my sister and I jumped in and all but one picture came out perfectly as we weren't ready for the last one. I had some really funky pineapple glasses ready for that one too!</span></div>
<div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><br /></span>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><b><i>Graduation is such a momentous moment<br />and I wish you all educational success in the present and future.<br />It can be hard, stressful and sometimes even feel pointless but let me tell you,<br />the hassle is most definitely worth the tassle!</i></b></span></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><i><b>And honey, I should see you in a cap and gown!</b></i></span><br />
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><b><i>Persevere and keep smiling xxx </i></b></span></div>

          <div class="date-posts">
        
<div class='post-outer'>
<div class='post hentry' itemprop='blogPost' itemscope='itemscope' itemtype='http://schema.org/BlogPosting'>
<meta content='http://do1p19p9dimlg.cloudfront.net/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/24-Really...-wow.gif' itemprop='image_url'/>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://skeenakeepsmiling.blogspot.co.uk/2014/07/footie-took-over-my-life.html'>Footie Took Over My Life!</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'><div class='post-share-buttons goog-inline-block'>
<a class='goog-inline-block share-button sb-email' href='http://www.blogger.com/share-post.g?blogID=6096219630196850848&postID=8467084161672732568&target=email' target='_blank' title='Email This'><span class='share-button-link-text'>Email This</span></a><a class='goog-inline-block share-button sb-blog' href='http://www.blogger.com/share-post.g?blogID=6096219630196850848&postID=8467084161672732568&target=blog' onclick='window.open(this.href, "_blank", "height=270,width=475"); return false;' target='_blank' title='BlogThis!'><span class='share-button-link-text'>BlogThis!</span></a><a class='goog-inline-block share-button sb-twitter' href='http://www.blogger.com/share-post.g?blogID=6096219630196850848&postID=8467084161672732568&target=twitter' target='_blank' title='Share to Twitter'><span class='share-button-link-text'>Share to Twitter</span></a><a class='goog-inline-block share-button sb-facebook' href='http://www.blogger.com/share-post.g?blogID=6096219630196850848&postID=8467084161672732568&target=facebook' onclick='window.open(this.href, "_blank", "height=430,width=640"); return false;' target='_blank' title='Share to Facebook'><span class='share-button-link-text'>Share to Facebook</span></a><a class='goog-inline-block share-button sb-pinterest' href='http://www.blogger.com/share-post.g?blogID=6096219630196850848&postID=8467084161672732568&target=pinterest' target='_blank' title='Share to Pinterest'><span class='share-button-link-text'>Share to Pinterest</span></a><div class='goog-inline-block dummy-container'><g:plusone source='blogger:blog:plusone' href='http://skeenakeepsmiling.blogspot.com/2014/07/footie-took-over-my-life.html' size='medium' width='300' annotation='inline'/></div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-8467084161672732568' itemprop='articleBody'>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
So very unlike myself, my week this week has been consumed by football. Intriguing how I have no interest in watching football throughout the rest of the year and the second the World Cup is on, I become the most patriotic football fan that is physically possible. But now that England were out, it was just a matter of watching how the pros do it!</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<b>Did 7-1 really happen? Like REALLY??</b></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
So I'm one of the people who missed the first half an hour of the Brazil Vs Germany match. But I was on twitter and my timeline was pretty much blowing up. My little sister came running into my room shouting the madness that was the 5-0 to Germany score and I was like<b> </b></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<img border="0" src="http://do1p19p9dimlg.cloudfront.net/magazine/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/24-Really...-wow.gif" /></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
(I feel like the more you stare at this gif, the more sarcastic Tennant seems!)</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Decided to be anti-antisocial and went downstairs where my family were watching the match and again similarly to myself, they too have a sudden patriotic boost when the World Cup greets us every four years.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
As I was watching, I did not think that anything else would happen for the rest of the match. I thought, "Yep, I've missed the good part. Germany went all out in the first couple of minutes and now they're just going to cool off.". Oh how I was wrong! They carried on fighting harder like they were still on nil points and hats off to them. 7-1 is an incredible score which I believe broke records. Unfortunately for Brazil, the host of the World Cup, my heart went out to them but a game's a game and Germany <i>definitely</i> brought their A-game; a well deserved win.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://ioneadwnews.files.wordpress.com/2014/07/brazil-10.jpg?w=640&amp;h=640" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="320" src="https://ioneadwnews.files.wordpress.com/2014/07/brazil-10.jpg?w=640&amp;h=640" width="320" /></a></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<b>Brutal and Long; Holland Vs Argentina</b></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
So Holland Vs Argentina were next in the limelight and after Brazil's thrashing by Germany's unsuspected win, there was a lot of eyes on this game. If it was going to be anything like the repeat of the Brazil Vs Germany match, nobody wanted to miss football history in the making and everybody was going to tune in - footie fan or not! And again, man was I wrong!</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
In a few words: </div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://www.discussny.com/attachments/entertainment/5389d1373822822-bbc-sherlock-tedious2.gif" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://www.discussny.com/attachments/entertainment/5389d1373822822-bbc-sherlock-tedious2.gif" /></a></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
The game just felt like a game of piggy-in-the-middle with the ball just being passed around the pitch back and forth with only too-close-for-comfort goals. But genuinely, I felt that bought teams were playing really well but they were just so well equally matched, which made it hard for either team to score. So expectantly, the game went into penalties even after an extra half an our overtime.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Let's just say, I would NOT want to be the goalie during penalties! The pressure of it all in a regular match must be intense anyway as it's all down to you who wins, so the pressure during the World Cup is pressure that I wound most definitely not be able to handle in any case.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<b>The End.</b></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<a href="http://img1.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb20140630010047/degrassi/images/d/d4/Final-Countdown.gif" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://img1.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb20140630010047/degrassi/images/d/d4/Final-Countdown.gif" height="185" width="320" /></a>&nbsp;</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
It indeed was the final countdown, and to put it simply Germany won the World Cup against Argentina and good on them! Out of the teams that I saw play in the World Cup, I definitely feel like Germany played like a team of football players rather than pinning their hopes on one good player what many believe is exactly what lead to Argentina's loss.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
Germany played strong throughout and what a great win!</div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
England have got four years to pull their socks up!</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>

<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-1125015029685524729' itemprop='articleBody'>
<b>The School Life</b><br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
As part of my SKE course that I'm currently on as a pre-PGCE Teacher Training course, we were sent out to local high schools just to get a feel of what it is like to be back to school again and what it was like back to school but from a teachers perspective. As it hasn't really been a while since I was in high school, it wasn't much of a change of how I remember it being. It's definitely a lot different from a teachers point of view now though as you have access to areas where I may have previous been denied. Also just for being someone other than a teacher at the school or a pupil, you get a lot of inquisitive looks and even questions.</div>
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-o1dv3rp0lPw/U9zJA_OG0iI/AAAAAAAAAZY/Qh8e18Geuug/s1600/when-someone-walks-into-your-classroom-funny-pictures.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-o1dv3rp0lPw/U9zJA_OG0iI/AAAAAAAAAZY/Qh8e18Geuug/s1600/when-someone-walks-into-your-classroom-funny-pictures.jpg" height="236" width="320" /></a></div>
<div>
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
I got to see different teachers teach their IT and Computing lessons which was a good insight to what I would be doing once I start teaching. But what I found a lot of fun was doing a Pupil Trail which basically meant I followed one pupil for the day to all their lessons. At first I was a bit nervous, I must admit, purely because I didn't want the pupil who I was paired up with to feel uncomfortable with me following them around to their lessons. But I soon felt this was not the case as they seemed more than happy to and was so lovely about it. I managed to see an RE, Maths, Music, Science and Food Technology lesson which all brought their very different methods of teaching which was really was great to see.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
These few days really reminded me the buzz of a classroom and it's pupils and the fun you could really create with them. And quite honestly, children are the most interesting people you will ever meet because they generate an insight to the world that us adults seem to forget.</div>
<br />
<b>#221back</b></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
This Friday I was supposed to publish a post on Sherlock a Series 1 on my Coach Potato page but I was just so busy with my mini school placement and so pooped out by the end of it, I just hadn't got any time to produce it. But don't worry, it's definitely on the to do list!</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Having said that, a very exciting Sherlock related announcement was made this week almost to cover up the lack of my Sherlock post! On Wednesday, Mark Gatiss and BBC One on twitter decided to be a &nbsp;little sneaky and quite the tease reminding us of Moriarty's "Did you miss me?" scene from the end of Series 3, Episode 3. And naturally, my whole twitter timeline was blowing up along with the Sherlock fandom.<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://sherlockdeduced.files.wordpress.com/2014/07/221back.jpg?w=774" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://sherlockdeduced.files.wordpress.com/2014/07/221back.jpg?w=774" height="185" width="320" /></a></div>
<br />
Soon after a long wait, and I mean loooong wait, it was announced that the official announcement would be announced the following day, which was Thursday, at 2:21pm. (I like what they did there! ;)). Unfortunately for me, I was away from any means of access to twitter at that time so had to wait till I got home to see what the announcement was.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
I jumped on twitter the second I could and it was announced that the game is on for Sherlock Series 4 for 2015 including a special. This basically means that we can definitely look forward to more Sherlock goodness but we do will have to wait at least another year and a half! The Special is assumed to be broadcasted around Christmas of 2015 followed by three episodes in the following start of the year similar to what we had for Series 3.<br />

</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-5683576435953922168' itemprop='articleBody'>
<div style="text-align: center;">
This week was full of this:</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<object class="BLOGGER-youtube-video" classid="clsid:D27CDB6E-AE6D-11cf-96B8-444553540000" codebase="http://download.macromedia.com/pub/shockwave/cabs/flash/swflash.cab#version=6,0,40,0" data-thumbnail-src="https://ytimg.googleusercontent.com/vi/zS1cLOIxsQ8/0.jpg" height="266" width="320"><param name="movie" value="https://youtube.googleapis.com/v/zS1cLOIxsQ8&amp;source=uds"><param name="bgcolor" value="#FFFFFF"><param name="allowFullScreen" value="true"><embed width="320" height="266" src="https://youtube.googleapis.com/v/zS1cLOIxsQ8&amp;source=uds" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" allowfullscreen="true"></object></div>
<br />
<b>Why Hello There 2:1 Honours</b><br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Monday, Tuesday and the start of Wednesday was pretty standard. Come Wednesday evening and my 2:1 degree classification was waiting for me! </div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
I wasn't expecting my results till the Friday but when a friend of mine who I saw on that Wednesday said that she got hers that morning, it suddenly got so nerve-racking! I checked online the minute she told me she had hers, but I had nothing. I just thought maybe their still processing everybody's results as we were from two different schools within the university.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
I got home and about 5pm-ish, I decided to go online and check again, and there was my results for each of my modules averaging out as a 2:1! It was a MASSIVE relief to see that final result. After three long years, I managed to come out of it with a decent mark considering at times I thought that surviving my undergraduate degree was the impossible.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://1mut.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/all-the-things-meme-collection-1mut.com-1.gif" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://1mut.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/all-the-things-meme-collection-1mut.com-1.gif" height="240" width="320" /></a></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Having a 2:1 confirms my place at my PGCE Teacher Training course for September and also confirms my scholarship that will pay my tuition fees for it! So for the next year, I am sorted and no longer in limbo!</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Also, having a 2:1 means that I have got my degree in BSc IT Management for Business. I have an actual degree. And if you've had a nosy at my <a href="http://skeenakeepsmiling.blogspot.co.uk/p/bucket-list.html" target="_blank">Bucket List</a>, one of the things on there is to graduate with a degree so I can actually tick something off! Well, half tick - BRING ON GRADUATION! :D</div>
<br />
<b>Not So Little, Little Sister</b><br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
I was an only child for the first 10 years
 of my childhood. Although I was happy in my own company for these 10 
years, there was no doubt that I longed to have a sibling. Everybody I 
knew had a brother or a sister or even two brothers and two sisters! I&#8217;d
 always hear the odd complains about sisters being annoying and brothers
 always tackling them to the ground but those things were brushed under 
the rug. From all the stories, all I processed was adventures to 
midnight Mcdonalds and secret ice cream trips on a cold night where 
otherwise would have been forbidden by their mums. It sounded AWESOME!</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
It is <b><i>awesome</i></b> <span class="wp-smiley emoji emoji-smile" title=":)">:)</span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://31.media.tumblr.com/25a8badc1d404529d0bf7a6e065b287b/tumblr_mue17yEKMv1s3h7qyo1_500.gif" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://31.media.tumblr.com/25a8badc1d404529d0bf7a6e065b287b/tumblr_mue17yEKMv1s3h7qyo1_500.gif" height="160" width="320" /></a></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span class="wp-smiley emoji emoji-smile" title=":)">&nbsp;</span>
</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
I have a little sister and she means the absolute world to me! There&#8217;s 10 years between us and most 
people would see that as a major flaw in the relationship, but let me 
tell you, I&#8217;m just as much as a big kid as she is! <span class="wp-smiley emoji emoji-bigsmile" title=":D">:D</span> I love her so much and I can not imagine being an only child for any longer than I was.
</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br />
And this week it was her birthday! #EmotionalBigSis I remember being her age and now she's that age and I'm like but I want you to stay small and fit in my pocket! I wish her the biggest of birthday wishes and hope that she has plenty more to come inshAllah!<br />
<br />
<div style="text-align: center;">
Party Time!</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://24.media.tumblr.com/tumblr_mcirmpIqB51qjln0ao4_500.gif" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://24.media.tumblr.com/tumblr_mcirmpIqB51qjln0ao4_500.gif" height="148" width="320" /></a></div>
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
If you have siblings, take care of them, 
respect them and love them, whether they are older than you, younger or 
even the same age! If you don&#8217;t have a sibling, all is not lost because 
your siblings are just <span class="skimlinks-unlinked">hidden.Your</span> siblings are just very good at disguises. Look at your closest friends and cousins. They are your siblings.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span class="wp-smiley emoji emoji-smile" title=":)"></span> </div>
<b>Ramadan Kareem!</b><br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Today is the first day of Ramadan. As a Muslim, one of the five basic pillars that we must follow is Sawm (Fasting). For the 30 days of the month of Ramadan, we fast from dawn till dusk. This means before dawn and the first prayer of the day, Fajr, we eat for the day (Suhoor/Sehri) and then we don't eat or drink for the rest of the day till the fourth prayer of the day, Maghrib, which is at dusk (Iftar).</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
It sounds difficult, and it can be for the first few days as your body is so used to eating throughout the day especially when you're bored but it is such a great month and a great experience as it re-calibrates the mind, faith and humbleness.</div>
<br />
<div style="text-align: center;">
As the days are longer in the summer, the fasts are also longer.</div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<img border="0" src="http://wp.patheos.com.s3.amazonaws.com/blogs/altmuslim/files/2013/07/Ramadan-meme_summer-300x225.jpg" /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
It is a month of giving and charity where the importance of these are amplified. We may feel hungry or thirsty for the day but I am so grateful that I have food and water at the end of my fast where others around the world won't. Fasting is an experience that I would recommend to anyone because it really puts life into a grateful perspective. </div>
<br />
<div style="text-align: center;">
<i><b>And on that lovely note, I wish everyone a fulfilling Ramadan!</b></i></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<i><b>Keep Smiling,</b></i></div>

<div class='post-header-line-1'><div class='post-share-buttons goog-inline-block'>

<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-8222724978015483385' itemprop='articleBody'>
<b>Teacher-To-Be</b><br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
This week I began my Pre-PGCE Subject Knowledge Enhancement Course. It's basically a short course for a few weeks that will top up my knowledge in the areas of Computing and IT as that's the subject that I will hopefully be qualifying to teach in.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br />
There's currently a huge demand for Computing teachers in particular as the skill to be able to program is becoming more desirable by the day. Here in the UK, the Government have even gone as far as claiming this year to be the Year of Code supported by initiatives like the <a href="http://uk.code.org/" target="_blank">Hour of Code</a>.<br />
<br />
There's also a massive push to get girls into computing as computing is a very male dominated subject and it really shouldn't be. To put things a little into perspective, in this course that I've started this week, there are only four of us females with about fourteen males. So to say that we're outnumbered is a mega understatement.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
I've always been passionate about teaching and IT has always been one of those things that I have just always had an interest in. The computing and the programming, I'm not so confident in but it's definitely something I'm excited to learn more about and this course has already done me wonders. This week we have learnt the basic of <a href="http://scratch.mit.edu/" target="_blank">Scratch</a> (MIT Children's Programming Software) and <a href="https://www.python.org/" target="_blank">Python</a> (which I can confirm is as scary as it sounds!).</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://images-blogger-opensocial.googleusercontent.com/gadgets/proxy?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.fractuslearning.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2012%2F02%2FIT_teacher.jpg&amp;container=blogger&amp;gadget=a&amp;rewriteMime=image%2F*" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://www.fractuslearning.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/IT_teacher.jpg" height="275" width="400" />&nbsp;</a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
(A little dated seeing as schools are phasing out IT and phasing in Computing!) </div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Apart from the academic side, everyone is so lovely at the course and we all get along quite well already which helps in an environment where we are all prone to start yelling verbal abuse at our laptops!</div>
<b><br /></b>
<b><b>Slow Clap for England...</b>&nbsp;</b></div>
<div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-N1v81ERl-Z8/U680Fpmr0WI/AAAAAAAAAWU/Zw_OFA9xIhY/s1600/football+meme.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-N1v81ERl-Z8/U680Fpmr0WI/AAAAAAAAAWU/Zw_OFA9xIhY/s1600/football+meme.jpg" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
Oh dear. Need I say more?</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: justify;">
To add to the embarrassment of losing against Uruguay, we still have to play Costa Rica even though we are definitely out of the World Cup. Plus I like how everyone was still hoping that we'd be able to squeeze our way into the next round through the outcome of the Uraguay Vs Costa Rica game.</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: justify;">
And the amount if memes I've seen about England's performance is just amazing! Haha, it's always good to be able to laugh at yourselves!</div>
<br />
<b>Open Day</b><br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
My feet hurt, but I wouldn't not work at my university's open days! So on Friday and Saturday we were blessed with beautiful weather for our open days for our University.As I've mentioned in previous posts, I'm a Student Ambassador for my University which allows me to work at any events that the University may hold; in this case it was their Open Days.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
I've worked on their open days in the past and they have always been a lot of fun. For all of the past Open Days that I have worked on, I have always been selected to do Campus Tours which is a tour of the university main campus pointing out the main buildings and facilities we have. I would get a headset microphone which would always make me feel like a 90's popstar!</div>
<br />
<div style="text-align: center;">
Pointing out buildings like:</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://richglare.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Britney.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://richglare.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Britney.jpg" height="263" width="320" /></a></div>
<br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
One fact that I always love to include is that Benedict Cumberbatch is an Alumni of the University whenever we pass the Music and Drama Building. I always have people's ears perk up at the sound of his name with a few "Ooo really??"'s. And I always like to have a bit of audience participation asking "So Benedict Cumberbatch is famously known for playing..." and I wait for an answer. Sometimes when nobody answers, I'm like "Right, all of your homework tonight is to watch Sherlock. It's mandatory!". Always gets a bit of a smile and a giggle!<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-NUa3vRUzBxo/U8EPWl1scUI/AAAAAAAAAYc/dnkrU3hYx4E/s1600/sherlock+fandom+uniform.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-NUa3vRUzBxo/U8EPWl1scUI/AAAAAAAAAYc/dnkrU3hYx4E/s1600/sherlock+fandom+uniform.jpg" height="259" width="320" /></a></div>
</div>
This time, I was actually based as part of the Student Accommodation team processing people in the queues who wanted to go on a Student Accommodation tour and walking them to the coaches that would take them there. At first I was a bit gutted that I didn't get my usual Campus Tour Guide role but liked that I could do something a little different for my last Open Day that I would work on!<br />
<br />
Plus they had sweets and lollipops so all was not lost!<br />
<div style="text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<i><b>That's me for the week!</b></i></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<i><b>I hope the football wasn't too disappointing for you!</b></i></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<i><b>Keep Smiling,</b></i></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<i><b>Skeena xxx</b></i></div>
