<br><div class="title"><h1>SWEATER WEATHER</h1></div><p><img alt="image" src="http://imageshack.com/a/img819/8448/n6cq.png" width="700px"/></p>
<p>(Top from <a href="http://www.topshop.com/" target="_blank">Topshop</a>, Shorts from <a href="http://www.forever21.com/" target="_blank">Forever 21</a>, Shoes from <a href="https://www.facebook.com/pages/Nine-West.../200296120035129" target="_blank">Nine West</a>, Bag from <a href="http://www.calvinklein.com/" target="_blank">Calvin Klein</a>, Earrings, necklace and hair accessory from <a href="https://www.facebook.com/AuthenticGlamorosa" target="_blank">Glamorosa</a>)</p>
<p><img alt="image" src="http://imageshack.com/a/img856/770/hmxw.png" width="700px"/></p>
<p>2 weeks of school and I honestly haven’t done anything productive yet. I think I’m still on vacation mode because my mind won’t process anything I’ve been learning in school. I was given loads of reading homework but I honestly didn’t take much time reading and trying to understand them. Either I go to the mall with some of my friends to eat or window shop, or I stay in to doze off after attending my classes and watch How I Met Your Mother or local television shows all night.</p>
<p><img alt="image" src="http://imageshack.com/a/img849/6788/vxtl.png" width="700px"/></p>
<p>One of the reasons why I’m still not over being a couch potato all day or just chilling around somewhere else is probably the suspension of classes just 2 days after our first day in school because of a typhoon. It is expected during the first week not to do anything huge or important in class so I didn’t really worry about anything school-related. I just stayed in and lived my lazy life. But whatever, I know I have to deal with all these things and do all my responsibilities as a student. So, yeah.</p>
<p><img alt="image" src="http://imageshack.com/a/img841/5731/xfjb.png" width="700px"/></p>
<p>Well, anyway&#8230;.. I&#8217;m on my model status.. Lol joke =))</p>
<p>So, that&#8217;s me. I&#8217;m one hell of a procrastinator. I&#8217;m actually blogging tonight because I don&#8217;t want to face my school notes yet. Haha! I don&#8217;t know why I like cramming and then work under pressure. Maybe because my brain is focused and is forced to think so all the ideas come rushing in. But I promise to study after posting this, okay! Hahaha.</p>
<p><img alt="image" src="http://imageshack.com/a/img842/2686/5p69.png" width="700px"/></p>
<p>Sweater weather is on! I honestly hate it when it rains, especially when I&#8217;m out. The only advantage I see is that the plants all over the place get watered, plus the cold breeze the rain brings. It&#8217;s been really hot here in the Philippines during summer, so it&#8217;s really a relief when the rainy season has come. We just pray that it doesn&#8217;t rain hard and nobody gets victimized by the strong rain and wind.</p>
<p><img alt="image" src="http://imageshack.com/a/img855/5886/ohkn.png" width="700px"/></p>
<p><img alt="image" src="http://imageshack.com/a/img834/2892/mv9m.png" width="700px"/></p>
<p>This cropped knitted sweater (which was on sale when I got it, yaaa) lets me cover up but doesn&#8217;t suffocate my body.
