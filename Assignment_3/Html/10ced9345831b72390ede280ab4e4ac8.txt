The Malden publicity machine has developed a few more cogs and is running more smoothly than I'd ever dared expect. This is entirely due to other people - so thank you other people. I'm therefore going to stick to the part over which I have most control, the actual writing.<br />
<br />
<i>Extra-Curricular </i>is finished! If you don't know (and there aren't any overwhelming reasons why you should), it's the fifth in my Gavin McClain series and, as it turns out, the longest by a country mile - the initial draft stands at somewhere over 58k. I'm planning on a November publication date, which will give me vast tracts of time to make sure that it's as good as it can be. It took longer to write than I'd ever intended, but amazingly the completed draft is pretty coherent. The good news, for those who keep coming back to my work, is that as well as being a genuine STORY, it's also a MASSIVE SEX-FEST. I mean there's a truckload of dirty sex action in this tale and it's all kinds of taboo-bustingly wrong. (Not icky taboos either - fun ones. Malden kind of wrong, I promise you, and in spades.)<br />
<br />
My worry, having written it in several concerted bursts over a ten month period, was that it wouldn't hold together as a single read. Well I ran it past my long-time erotic co-conspirator, good friend and most honest critic Mia Natasha this week and her verdict was that it works. She read it in one four and a half-hour sitting, so she tells me, and was positive enough to put a big fat smile on my face. Her thumbs-up review is a serious relief. It's particularly good to know that my characterising of Gavin is on track and I should keep taking him where I'm taking him, i.e. to all kinds of naughty places.<br />

</h3>

<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-2741118587348341123' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">

<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-Uk6akEJXjk8/U9InUlWDsxI/AAAAAAAADUM/KNOSn0ubw-c/s1600/Thursday+Taster+image.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-Uk6akEJXjk8/U9InUlWDsxI/AAAAAAAADUM/KNOSn0ubw-c/s1600/Thursday+Taster+image.jpg" height="111" width="320" /></a></div>
I'm a day late and for that I apologise, but I'm clawing my way back from the mid-summer work malaise and taking that into account I hope you'll forgive me. You can access all this week's 'tasters', from a range of talented writers, by following this link:<br />
<a class="bitmark-shortlink" href="http://bit.ly/1nFXbSL" style="color: #222222; cursor: pointer; display: inline-block; font-family: 'Source Sans Pro', sans-serif; font-size: 14px; height: 23px; line-height: 21px; margin: 3px 20px 0px 0px; overflow: hidden; padding: 0px; text-decoration: none; text-overflow: ellipsis; white-space: nowrap; width: 140px;"><span class="protocol" style="display: inline !important; height: 1px; left: -9999px; line-height: 1px; margin: 0px; overflow: hidden; padding: 0px; position: absolute; top: -9999px; width: 1px;">http://</span></a><br />
<a class="bitmark-shortlink" href="http://bit.ly/1nFXbSL" style="color: #222222; cursor: pointer; display: inline-block; font-family: 'Source Sans Pro', sans-serif; font-size: 14px; height: 23px; line-height: 21px; margin: 3px 20px 0px 0px; overflow: hidden; padding: 0px; text-decoration: none; text-overflow: ellipsis; white-space: nowrap; width: 140px;">bit.ly/1nFXbSL</a><br />
My Gavin McClain story <i>Twenty-One</i>, the fourth in the series, will be published on Tuesday 5th August. It tells the story of Brooke, a girl who takes on more than she can handle on the twenty-first birthday when dared to hit on a dark and debonair Englishman at a New York night spot. You already know his name...<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-qZRPVwaLtBM/U9Il2Zv-BFI/AAAAAAAADUA/sKPNzON_rC8/s1600/Twenty-One+Book+Cover.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-qZRPVwaLtBM/U9Il2Zv-BFI/AAAAAAAADUA/sKPNzON_rC8/s1600/Twenty-One+Book+Cover.JPG" height="320" width="240" /></a></div>
<br />
<div class="Style3CxSpFirst">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">She&#8217;d given
him her animated, smiling, blue-eyed best, but eventually her words tailed away
sadly. Perhaps such tactics only worked with nave college types. She released
his wrist abashed at her own recklessness.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpFirst">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">But there was
a certain thawing of his flinty visage. His eyes took her in properly for the
first time. In fact they went on a brief tour and took her all in before
settling on her face. &#8220;Twenty-first, eh? So this is a rite of passage.&#8221;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">&#8220;A&#8230;&#8221;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">&#8220;A coming of
age.&#8221;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">&#8220;Yeah, I
know. I know what a rite of passage is.&#8221; Her eyes dropped with a shyness she
hadn&#8217;t felt in years. &#8220;I guess that&#8217;s what my friend had in mind.&#8221; God, they&#8217;d
been Kimber&#8217;s exact words.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">&#8220;So you&#8217;re
swallowing all your trepidation and approaching what your friend no doubt
described as &#8216;a real man&#8217;, or something similar.&#8221; His voice has softened and
she raised her eyes to his, trying to transcend her girlishness. His features
had fully relaxed as well, to something amiable. Playful. &#8220;Yet there I was,&#8221; he
said, &#8220;treating you like a schoolgirl. ,Very lacking in chivalry. What can I
get you to drink?&#8221;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">Brooke&#8217;s
relief was like a rush of cooling water. &#8220;I&#8217;ll have a mint julep. Thank you.&#8221;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">He raised a
hand and drew immediate attention from the girl serving closest. &#8220;Mint julep
for the young lady.&#8221; He smiled at Brooke and she tried, quite heroically she
thought, not to simper. &#8220;A twenty-first birthday should be celebrated in style.
My nephew turned twenty-one a couple of months back. I took quite a deal of
trouble to make it memorable.&#8221;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">&#8220;That&#8217;s very
family-spirited of you.&#8221; God, he had a grown nephew, this man. It served to
emphasize the age-gap. This was a man of maturity, of responsibility. &#8220;Still,&#8221;
she said, &#8220;I don&#8217;t mean to take up your time. You looked kind of preoccupied
sitting there.&#8221;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">&#8220;Last-minute
prep for a business meeting tomorrow morning. It&#8217;s the reason I&#8217;m in the
States.&#8221;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">&#8220;Oh wow, I&#8217;m
sorry. If I&#8217;d known &#8230;&#8221;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">&#8220;You&#8217;d have
done exactly what you did,&#8221; he said, and she blushed to be called on her
disingenuous comment. &#8220;And you&#8217;d have been right to. If I wasn&#8217;t open to
distraction I&#8217;d have stayed in my hotel room to work.&#8221;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">&#8220;So I&#8217;m a
distraction?&#8221; she asked, brushing a loose strand of hair behind her ear and
daring to look straight into his dark eyes.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">&#8220;A pleasant
distraction,&#8221; he told her, intensifying her blush. &#8220;Going by the name of &#8230;&#8221;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">&#8220;Brooke. I&#8217;m
Brooke.&#8221;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">&#8220;I tell you
what, Brooke&#8722;you help me clear my head of everything business-related and I&#8217;ll
help you celebrate your birthday. How does that sound?&#8221;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">She almost
let the mint julep spill as he handed it to her. &#8220;It&#8217;s a deal &#8230;&#8221;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">&#8220;Gavin,&#8221; he
said, taking her slim hand gently in his massive grip.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="Style3CxSpMiddle">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<br />
<div class="Style3CxSpLast">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">&#8220;I love that
name., Pleased to meet you, Gavin.&#8221;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<br /></div>
