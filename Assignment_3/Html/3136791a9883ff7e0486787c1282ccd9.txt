<p>The user known as <b>Shran</b> has been a member of Memory Alpha since June of <a href="/wiki/2005" title="2005">2005</a>. His real name is <b>Charles</b> (born February 13). His username was chosen in honor of the <a href="/wiki/Andorian" title="Andorian">Andorian</a> character of the <a href="/wiki/Thy%27lek_Shran" title="Thy'lek Shran">same name</a>, played by <a href="/wiki/Jeffrey_Combs" title="Jeffrey Combs">Jeffrey Combs</a> on <i><a href="/wiki/Star_Trek:_Enterprise" title="Star Trek: Enterprise">Star Trek: Enterprise</a></i>.
</p><p>Born in <a href="/wiki/Virginia" title="Virginia">Virginia</a>, Charles has been a <i><a href="/wiki/Star_Trek" title="Star Trek">Star Trek</a></i> fan since the early <a href="/wiki/1990s" title="1990s">1990s</a>, beginning with <i><a href="/wiki/Star_Trek:_The_Next_Generation" title="Star Trek: The Next Generation">Star Trek: The Next Generation</a></i>, when he was living in <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>. He has since followed the franchise faithfully, having seen every episode of <a href="/wiki/Star_Trek:_The_Original_Series" title="Star Trek: The Original Series">the original series</a>, <i>The Next Generation</i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Star_Trek:_Deep_Space_Nine" title="Star Trek: Deep Space Nine">Deep Space Nine</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Star_Trek:_Enterprise" title="Star Trek: Enterprise">Enterprise</a></i>, most of <i><a href="/wiki/Star_Trek:_Voyager" title="Star Trek: Voyager">Voyager</a></i>, and owns all ten <a href="/wiki/Star_Trek_films" title="Star Trek films"><i>Star Trek</i> films</a>. 
</p><p>Charles considers himself a die-hard "Trekker" (or <a href="/wiki/Trekkie" title="Trekkie">Trekkie</a>, whichever you consider to be the more obsessed of the two). He believes in the optimistic vision created by <a href="/wiki/Gene_Roddenberry" title="Gene Roddenberry">Gene Roddenberry</a>, and is obsessed with creating explanations for apparent continuity errors to ensure the <a href="/wiki/Star_Trek" title="Star Trek">Star Trek</a> timeline remains internally consistent. Most importantly, however, he enjoys the good, high-quality entertainment which <i>Trek</i> almost always offers. His love of <i>Trek</i> encompasses all ten <a href="/wiki/Film" title="Film" class="mw-redirect">feature films</a> and all of the <a href="/wiki/Television" title="Television">television</a> series (with the exception of <a href="/wiki/Star_Trek:_The_Animated_Series" title="Star Trek: The Animated Series">the animated series</a>, which he has seen very little of and does not consider fully <a href="/wiki/Canon" title="Canon">canon</a>).
</p><p>He is an alum of Christopher Newport University, where he majored first in Acting and then in Theater Studies. A recent graduate, he currently does very little with the exception surfing the net and watching television (looking for work in NYC figures in there somewhere). Besides <i>Star Trek</i>, he is a huge fanatic of film and TV in general, and is what many people would consider a "film encyclopedia," as he has a vast amount of trivia about all types of movies: the old, the new, and the yet-to-be-released.
</p><p>Prior to creating an account on June 18th, 2005, Charles contributed numerous times to Memory Alpha. Pages he created as an anonymous user include <a href="/wiki/Clara_Sutter" title="Clara Sutter">Clara Sutter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Sutter" title="Daniel Sutter">Daniel Sutter</a>. He also contributed to the pages for <a href="/wiki/Jonathan_Archer" title="Jonathan Archer">Jonathan Archer</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_Federation_of_Planets" title="United Federation of Planets">United Federation of Planets</a>, <a href="/wiki/Klingon" title="Klingon">Klingon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Klingon_Empire" title="Klingon Empire">Klingon Empire</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Isabella" title="Isabella">Isabella</a>.
</p><p>Charles had a less-than-auspicious start as a named member of Memory Alpha. Unfamiliar with the rules and policies of this wiki, he began by adding useless nonsense to a few articles and also blanked articles. However, he was quickly set straight by the likes of <a href="/wiki/User:Gvsualan" title="User:Gvsualan">Alan del Beccio</a>, and has since become a valued member of Memory Alpha &#8211; or so he likes to think. He also likes to think that he's become an expert on the Star Trek universe; however, he admits that he is always learning new, interesting details... but he wouldn't have it any other way.
</p><p>Charles must be doing something right, since he is now an <a href="/wiki/Memory_Alpha:Administrators" title="Memory Alpha:Administrators">administrator</a> on Memory Alpha. He was initially <a href="/wiki/Memory_Alpha:Nominations_for_administratorship" title="Memory Alpha:Nominations for administratorship">nominated for administatorship</a> by <a href="/wiki/User:Tim_Thomason" title="User:Tim Thomason">Tim Thomason</a> on September 24th and was formally "initiated" by <a href="/wiki/User:Cid_Highwind" title="User:Cid Highwind">Cid Highwind</a> on November 4th.
</p><p>On 15 August 2007, Charles became a volunteer writer for <a rel="nofollow" class="external text exitstitial" href="http://www.trekmovie.com">TrekMovie.com</a>. Charles assisted the site's founder and operator, Anthony Pascale, with the writing and posting of news from sources outside of trekmovie.com. You can see his first news story, edited by Mr. Pascale, <a rel="nofollow" class="external text exitstitial" href="http://trekmovie.com/2007/08/15/cruise-crowe-rumors-find-new-life-in-brit-tabloids/">here</a>. Charles wrote the site's CelebWatch column but began been contributing less frequently within a few years due to his busy schedule and factors related to the site. He no longer writes for the site, but would be willing to do so if asked. 
</p><p>Charles was the first person to suggest <a href="/wiki/Chris_Pine" title="Chris Pine">Chris Pine</a> for the role of <a href="/wiki/James_T._Kirk" title="James T. Kirk">James T. Kirk</a> in <a href="/wiki/2009_productions" title="2009 productions">2009</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Star_Trek_(film)" title="Star Trek (film)"><span style="cursor: help;" title="Star Trek">Star Trek</span></a></i>. He brought Pine up as a possibility at TrekMovie.com (see message 104 <a rel="nofollow" class="external text exitstitial" href="http://trekmovie.com/2007/07/27/tabloid-rumor-robbie-williams-as-kirk#104">here</a>). Among the people who read TrekMovie.com is the film's co-writer, <a href="/wiki/Roberto_Orci" title="Roberto Orci">Roberto Orci</a>. Less than three months later, Pine was signed to the role. Coincidence? Maybe... but you never know.&#160;;)
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="The_Pink_Skin_with_a_Blue_Skin.27s_Name">The Pink Skin with a Blue Skin's Name</span></h2>
<dl><dd><i>Shran does what he does because he loves you, just as he loves all of God's children. Yet, like a spoiled child, you lash out at him out of spite because you have been led astray. For that I say: shame on you, dear sir, shame on you!</i> --<a href="/wiki/User:Gvsualan" title="User:Gvsualan">Alan del Beccio</a> to a vandal who apparently has a grudge against me for doing my job

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Currently_on_Memory_Alpha">Currently on Memory Alpha</span></h2>
<p>The majority of my work has been in expanding those articles for <i>Trek</i> actors and actresses. I take an extreme interest in what performers have done outside of <i>Trek</i>, and research as much as possible to point out their major works and all the appearances with other <i>Trek</i> performers I can find. For a list of currently unwritten performer pages, check out <a href="/wiki/Memory_Alpha:List_of_unwritten_performer_articles" title="Memory Alpha:List of unwritten performer articles">this page here</a>, initially created by <a href="/wiki/User:Tough_Little_Ship" title="User:Tough Little Ship">Tough Little Ship</a>.
</p><p>I'll also be working on the performers pages for each of the shows (with the exception of <a href="/wiki/TAS" title="TAS" class="mw-redirect">TAS</a>) and will also be editing and creating pages for individual performers. So far I've done a <i>lot</i> of 'em, although only the biggest edits &amp; additions will be listed in my "major contribution" list below. In addition, I am currently compiling and adding series credits for each individual season of each show. Thus far, I've added complete credits for <a href="/wiki/DS9_Season_1" title="DS9 Season 1">DS9 seasons one</a> through <a href="/wiki/DS9_Season_4" title="DS9 Season 4">four</a>, <a href="/wiki/ENT_Season_4" title="ENT Season 4">ENT Season 4</a>, and <a href="/wiki/VOY_Season_2" title="VOY Season 2">VOY Season 2</a>. 
</p><p>I will also be responsible for updating the "Latest News" section on the <a href="/wiki/Main_Page" title="Main Page" class="mw-redirect">Main Page</a>. In addition, I will take it upon myself to update the <i><a href="/wiki/Star_Trek_(film)" title="Star Trek (film)"><span style="cursor: help;" title="Star Trek">Star Trek</span></a></i> when new information is released. I have already added numerous bits of information over the months and recently did an extensive overhaul to make it look more like an encyclopedia article rather than a report. It should be noted, however, that much of the information there is courtesy of <a rel="nofollow" class="external text exitstitial" href="http://www.trekmovie.com">TrekMovie.com</a>, which is the best <i>Star Trek</i> news site out there, IMHO. Actually, it's not just an opinion... it's fact. Deal with it.&#160;;)
</p><p>My other duties on MA include deleting old IP user talk pages (a task shared with <a href="/wiki/User:Gvsualan" title="User:Gvsualan">Alan</a> and, sometimes, <a href="/wiki/User:Jaz" title="User:Jaz">Jaz</a>), deleting those pages and images voted for deletion <a href="/wiki/Memory_Alpha:Pages_for_deletion" title="Memory Alpha:Pages for deletion">here</a>, <a href="/wiki/Memory_Alpha:Files_for_deletion" title="Memory Alpha:Files for deletion">here</a>, <a href="/wiki/Memory_Alpha:Pages_for_immediate_deletion" title="Memory Alpha:Pages for immediate deletion" class="mw-redirect">here</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Memory_Alpha:Possible_copyright_infringements" title="Memory Alpha:Possible copyright infringements">here</a> (I also clean up those pages). I also help take care of <a href="/wiki/Special:WhatLinksHere/Template:Merge_with" title="Special:WhatLinksHere/Template:Merge with">those pages to be merged</a> and I also update the <a href="/wiki/Template:Unwrittentopics" title="Template:Unwrittentopics">wanted pages template</a>. Naturally, I'm not the <i>only</i> one who does these things &#8211; this is a community effort after all &#8211; but they are among my own "personal duties", if you will.
